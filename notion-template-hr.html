Frequently Asked Questions

Questions From HR Professionals

How does the hiring pipeline work in Notion?

The hiring pipeline is a kanban database with stages: Applied → Phone Screen → Interview → Skills Assessment → Offer → Hired. Each candidate card holds their resume link, interview notes, feedback, and decision rationale — all in one place, visible to everyone involved in the process.

Can I create a standardized onboarding checklist for every new hire?

Yes. AgencyOS includes an onboarding checklist template that you duplicate for each new hire. It covers pre-start setup, first-day logistics, first-week milestones, and 30/60/90-day check-ins — assigned to the right people so nothing is forgotten.

Does this work for HR at a small company or only large teams?

AgencyOS is ideal for HR professionals at small and mid-size companies — typically 5 to 200 employees. It's flexible enough to adapt to your current team size and scales as you grow without needing to rebuild your systems.

How do I track PTO and time-off requests in Notion?

The PTO tracker is a database with each employee's accrued days, used days, and remaining balance — updated as requests are submitted. You can filter by employee, month, or department to get the visibility you need at review time.
