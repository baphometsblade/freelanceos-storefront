Section 2

ConvertKit Step-by-Step Setup

Follow these steps in order after signing up for a free Kit/ConvertKit account. This will connect your free-toolkit.html lead magnet form to the automated 5-email sequence.

  1. Create your account and confirm your email — Go to kit.com, sign up, and verify your sender email address. Use markmma1985@gmail.com or a custom domain address (recommended: hello@yourdomain.com).
  2. Create a new Form — In the dashboard, go to Grow → Landing Pages & Forms → New Form. Choose "Inline" form type. Name it: Free Toolkit - Lead Magnet. Collect: First Name + Email. Save the form.
  3. Get your Form embed code or API ID — On the form settings page, click Embed. Copy the Form ID (a number like 1234567). You'll use this to connect your HTML form via ConvertKit's API when you're ready to wire up live email collection.
  4. Connect the form to a Sequence — While on your Form page, click Settings → Incentive. Set the incentive email to deliver your Gumroad link: https://markmma1985.gumroad.com/l/fvjgxo. Then under After subscribing, set Add to sequence and select the sequence you'll create in the next step.
  5. Create the Welcome Sequence — Go to Send → Sequences → New Sequence. Name it: FreelanceOS Free Toolkit Welcome. Add 5 emails using the full copy in Section 3 below. Set email timings: Email 1 = immediately, Email 2 = day 2, Email 3 = day 4, Email 4 = day 7, Email 5 = day 14.
  6. Set up the Automation trigger — Go to Automate → Visual Automations → New Automation. Add trigger: Subscribes to a form → select Free Toolkit - Lead Magnet. Add action: Add to sequence → select FreelanceOS Free Toolkit Welcome. Activate the automation.
  7. Tag subscribers for segmentation — In the automation, add a second action after the form subscribe: Add tag → create tag source: free-toolkit. This lets you later segment these leads from paid customers or other lead sources.
  8. Wire up your HTML form (when ready) — Replace the current form JavaScript in free-toolkit.html with a fetch call to ConvertKit's API: POST https://api.convertkit.com/v3/forms/{form_id}/subscribe with fields email, first_name, and your api_key. On success, show the existing success state UI.
  9. Test the full flow — Subscribe with a test email address. Confirm the incentive email arrives within 5 minutes with the Gumroad link, then confirm Email 1 of the sequence sends immediately after that. Check that the subscriber appears tagged in your ConvertKit dashboard.
