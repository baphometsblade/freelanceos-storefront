The Problem

The Booking Chaos Eating Into Your Grooming Revenue

📄

Pet preferences and grooming notes scattered across texts and memory — a client's dog is nervous around dryers and needs hand towel drying only, but that detail lives in a year-old text message rather than a profile you check before every appointment.

🚌

Mobile routes planned day-of with no efficiency tracking — driving across town to reach a single appointment between two others in the same neighborhood because there's no system for batching bookings by geographic zone.

💰

Revenue tracked by mental math at day's end — tips not logged, add-on upsells not tracked, and no visibility into whether Tuesdays or Fridays are actually more profitable until tax season forces the question.

🥀

Supply inventory managed by checking the shelf — running out of de-shedding shampoo mid-week, discovering a detangler is nearly empty when a matted Doodle is on the table, and ordering supplies reactively instead of proactively.

📈

No rebooking rate visibility — some clients come every 4 weeks like clockwork; others disappear after their first visit. Without tracking who has rebooked and who hasn't, it's impossible to identify and address the retention problem.

What's Inside

Six Modules Built for Pet Grooming Professionals

🐶

Client & Pet Profile

Owner name, contact, address (for mobile routing), pet name, species, breed, color/markings, size (toy/small/medium/large/XL), age, coat type (double/single/curly/wire/smooth/silky/corded), grooming style preference (breed standard/teddy bear/puppy cut/show trim/low-maintenance), specific care instructions, vet name and clinic, vaccination status confirmed, known health notes (senior pet/hip dysplasia/skin sensitivities — for safety), grooming history, frequency, preferred groomer.

📅

Appointment Scheduler & Service Log

Per appointment: client name, pet name, service booked (full groom/bath and dry/trim/nail clip/teeth brushing/de-shedding/flea treatment/mat removal/hand-stripping/creative grooming), appointment date and time, duration, price, add-ons on the day, payment received, payment method (cash/card/Venmo/Zelle/PayPal), tip, next appointment booked (y/n), notes for next visit. Your complete appointment and revenue history.

🚌

Mobile Grooming Route Planner

Day of week, geographic zone or area (batch by neighborhood), client names and addresses on that route, estimated drive time between appointments, appointment times, mileage for the route, fuel cost estimate, parking notes, access notes (gated community codes, apartment buzzer). Route efficiency tracking: fewer miles driven, more appointments per day, higher daily revenue without longer hours.

✂

Service Menu & Pricing System

Full groom by size bracket (toy/small/medium/large/XL), bath and blow-dry, nail trim, nail grinding, teeth brushing, ear cleaning, de-shedding treatment, flea/tick treatment, mat removal (per hour or per area), hand-stripping (by quote), creative/fashion grooming, puppy's first groom package. Coat condition surcharges: matted coat policy, double coat heavy shedding upgrade. Seasonal pricing adjustments.

📦

Product & Supply Inventory

Shampoos and conditioners (brand, coat type suited for, quantity, cost, reorder point), finishing sprays, detanglers, cologne/spritz, clippers (model, blade set, last sharpened, next due), scissors (straight/curved/thinning, last sharpened), dryers (stand/force/cage), grooming tables, nail clippers, styptic powder, ear cleaning solution, ear powder, toothbrush/toothpaste, grooming wipes, towels. Your supply inventory and reorder system.

💼

Grooming Business Dashboard

Total clients, appointments this week, revenue this week, monthly revenue, revenue by service type, average ticket size, tip percentage, mobile route efficiency (appointments/day × average ticket), top-selling services, repeat client rate, cancellation rate, no-show rate, product COGS, overhead (van insurance/fuel/salon rent/equipment/insurance), profit margin, annual revenue goal.
