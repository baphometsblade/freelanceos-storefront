Yes. CreatorHQ's multi-client content calendar shows all posts across all clients in one view, filterable by client, platform, status, and date. Each post links to the client record so you can navigate client-by-client or see the full picture at once.

Does the template include a client approval workflow?

Yes. The Client Approval Workflow tracks each piece of content through Draft to Submitted for Review to Approved to Scheduled to Published. Clients can be given a filtered share view to comment on content without seeing other clients' work.
