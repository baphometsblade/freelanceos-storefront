Frequently Asked Questions

Questions From Professional Resume Writers

How does the ATS optimization tracker work?

The ATS tracker records your target job titles, the primary keywords you extract from 3 or more target job descriptions (skills, certifications, tools, action verbs), a checklist of whether each keyword has been incorporated into the resume, the ATS test tool used (Jobscan, Resume Worded, Teal, SkillSyncer), and the ATS match score before and after optimization. It also includes a formatting checklist: no tables, no text boxes, no key information in headers or footers, no images, clean section headings that match ATS parsing expectations, and consistent date formatting throughout. This is your quality control gate before every resume leaves your desk — ensuring documents are not just well-written but actually parseable by the systems that will screen them first.

How does the document library help me work faster on new projects?

The document library stores your professional templates organized by industry and document type: resume templates by industry (tech, finance, healthcare, legal, creative, executive, federal), cover letter templates by situation, LinkedIn profile structure templates by seniority level, reference page template, bio template, and federal resume format for USAJobs. When a new project begins in a category you have worked in before, you retrieve the most relevant template and adapt it rather than starting from a blank document. Resume writers who maintain a template library complete projects faster, produce more consistent quality across similar engagements, and can take on higher volume without increasing revision rates — because the structural thinking is already done and stored.

Does the pricing tracker help me set rates for different seniority levels?

Yes. The pricing and package manager stores your fee structure by seniority tier and package type: entry-level (resume only $250-400; full package $500-700), mid-level ($350-600; full package $700-1,000), senior/manager ($500-900; full package $900-1,500), director/VP ($800-1,500; full package $1,500-2,500), C-suite/executive ($1,500-3,000+; full package $2,500-5,000+), federal resume ($500-900), rush fee policy by turnaround window, and premium positioning for certified professionals. Having pricing clearly documented prevents inconsistent quoting and makes it easy to raise rates systematically by tier as your credentials and reputation grow — without re-negotiating your entire pricing structure from scratch each time.

Can I track my professional certifications and CPD for renewal?

Yes. The business dashboard and continuing education log track certifications held (CPRW from PARW/CC, NCRW from NRWA, MRW and ACRW from CDI), CPD hours for each certification renewal cycle, training and workshops completed, and renewal deadlines. Professional resume writing credentials require ongoing continuing professional development — having your CPD hours logged and renewal dates visible means you never approach a renewal deadline without knowing exactly how many hours you still need to complete. The credential section also functions as professional positioning documentation when marketing your services to new clients.
