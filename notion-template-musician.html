Frequently Asked Questions

Questions From Musicians

Can I track both live gigs and studio sessions?

Yes. FreelanceOS Pro has separate databases for gig bookings and studio sessions. The Gig Tracker logs venue, date, set time, payment, and load-in details. The Studio Session Log tracks recording dates, engineer, tracks worked on, and session costs.

Does it include a setlist builder?

Yes. The Song Database links directly to your setlists. Build setlists by dragging songs into ordered lists, tag them by event type, and print or share them with bandmates in one click.

How do I track merchandise inventory?

The Merchandise Inventory database lets you log each product with SKU, stock quantity, price, sales channel, and reorder threshold. Automatic rollups show total inventory value and which items are running low.

Can I track streaming royalties and sync licensing separately?

Yes. The royalty tracking section separates streaming income (Spotify, Apple Music, etc.) from sync licensing, live performance royalties, and mechanical royalties so you can see exactly where your music income comes from.

Is this suitable for solo artists and bands?

Absolutely. Solo artists use it to manage their entire career solo. Bands can share the workspace as a team — split income tracking, assign responsibilities, and keep everyone aligned on gig logistics and rehearsal schedules.
