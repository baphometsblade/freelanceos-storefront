Frequently Asked Questions

Questions From Language Teachers

How do language teachers use Notion?

Language teachers use Notion as their teaching headquarters — a student database linked to a lesson plan library, a CEFR progress tracker, a curriculum builder, and a business dashboard all in one connected workspace. Instead of tracking 20 students across sticky notes, Google Sheets, and email threads, everything is searchable and linked. FreelanceOS Pro gives you a pre-built version of this system ready to duplicate in under an hour.

Can Notion track CEFR levels for language students?

Yes. FreelanceOS Pro includes a CEFR Progress Assessment Tracker where each student record stores their current level (A1 through C2), the date of their initial assessment, quarterly reassessment results broken down by skill (speaking fluency, listening comprehension, writing coherence, grammar accuracy, vocabulary range), and a plain-text progression log showing their level over time. For exam-prep students, it links directly to the Exam Prep Tracker.

Does this template work for teachers on italki and Preply?

Yes. The Student Progress Tracker includes a platform field with options for Zoom, Skype, italki, Preply, and in-person. The Business Dashboard compares revenue per hour across platforms so you can see whether your direct clients, italki sessions, or Preply marketplace bookings generate the best hourly rate — an essential insight for deciding where to invest your marketing and availability.

Can I build lesson plans and reuse them across students?

Yes. The Lesson Plan Library is a searchable database of reusable lesson templates — each with fields for target CEFR level, skill focus (speaking, listening, reading, writing, grammar, vocabulary, pronunciation), topic, materials, activity types, and post-lesson notes. You can filter by level or skill to find the right lesson for any student in seconds, then add session-specific notes without modifying the template itself.

How does the exam prep tracking work for IELTS and TOEFL students?

The Exam Prep Tracker stores each student's target exam (IELTS Academic, IELTS General, TOEFL iBT, Cambridge B2 First, C1 Advanced, DELF, DELE, JLPT, HSK), exam date, target score, mock test scores by section, weak areas identified, and practice resources. When you review progress each lesson, you update the mock scores and the tracker shows you which sections need the most work before exam day.
