FAQ

Common Questions

Do clients need a Notion account to view the portal?
No. Clients can view the portal in any browser with just a link — no Notion account or sign-up required. You share the page with "anyone with link" view-only access.
Can I create a separate portal for each client?
Yes. Duplicate the portal template for each new project or client. Each gets their own isolated page you can share individually. Takes about 30 seconds per client.
Can clients edit or leave comments in the portal?
With view-only sharing, clients can't edit. If you want clients to comment, you can enable "Allow comments" in Notion's sharing settings on a per-page basis.
Does this replace a project management tool like Asana?
For most freelancers, yes. FreelanceOS Pro's project tracker has Kanban, timeline, and milestone views. You likely don't need a separate tool for a 1-2 person operation.
