Frequently Asked Questions

Can I track policy details and renewal dates for clients?
Yes. FreelanceOS Pro includes a dedicated Policy Tracker and Renewal Calendar. Log every client policy with carrier, coverage amount, premium, and renewal date — then filter to see which renewals are coming up in the next 30, 60, or 90 days so you never miss a contact window.
How do I manage my lead pipeline in Notion?
The Lead Pipeline database lets you track every prospect from initial contact through needs analysis, quote, application, and placement. Set stage, priority, and follow-up date for each lead so nothing slips through the cracks and your conversion rate improves over time.
Does it help track commissions and income?
Yes. The Commission Log lets you record earned commissions by policy, carrier, and month. You can see at a glance which carriers and product lines drive the most revenue and forecast your income based on upcoming renewals and expected new business.
Can I manage referral partner relationships?
Absolutely. The Referral Partner Tracker is built for managing relationships with realtors, mortgage brokers, accountants, attorneys, and other centers of influence. Log every interaction, track referrals received, and plan your next touchpoint to keep relationships warm and productive.
How do I organize carrier and product information?
The Carrier & Product Database gives you one place to catalog every product you represent — with carrier name, commission rate, key eligibility criteria, and product notes. Link policies in your tracker back to the relevant carrier record for fast reference during client conversations and needs analyses.
