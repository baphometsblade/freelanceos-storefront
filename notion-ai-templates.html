Getting Started

How to Enable Notion AI in 3 Steps

Notion AI is an optional add-on. Here's how to activate it — takes less than 2 minutes.

  1. Go to Settings & Members in Your Notion Workspace

    Click your workspace name in the top-left of Notion, then select Settings & Members. Navigate to the Plans tab. You'll see your current plan and the option to add Notion AI.

  2. Add the Notion AI Add-On ($10/month)

    Click Add Notion AI and complete the billing step. Notion AI is $10/month per member and works on all plan tiers including the free plan. It's billed separately from your Notion subscription. Once activated, the /ai command becomes available everywhere.

  3. Duplicate FreelanceOS and Start Using AI Features

    Duplicate your FreelanceOS workspace into Notion. All AI commands, prompts, and database properties are pre-configured. Open any template and use /ai to access writing assistance, or click any AI property in a database to trigger auto-generation. You're set up in under 5 minutes.
