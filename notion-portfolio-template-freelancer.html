Can I share my Notion portfolio publicly with clients? ▼
Yes. Notion’s native share settings let you publish any page or gallery as a public link. The FreelanceOS portfolio template is designed with a gallery layout optimised for sharing — you can send a single URL to prospects so they browse your case studies without needing a Notion account.
What types of freelancers does this template work for? ▼
The template works for any service-based freelancer — designers, developers, copywriters, SEO consultants, video editors, social media managers, and more. Category tags let you filter and display only the relevant work for each prospect.
Does the portfolio connect to the rest of FreelanceOS? ▼
Yes. Each portfolio entry links directly to the corresponding client record in your CRM database, as well as related projects, invoices, and testimonials. Everything in FreelanceOS Pro is cross-linked so your work history is never siloed.
