Validate Ideas Before You Build Them

The Idea Scoring Matrix

Every idea in your Idea Backlog is rated on four dimensions (each 1-5). The system calculates a composite Validation Score (max 20). Ideas scoring 15+ are flagged Build Now. Below 10 - auto-archived. The four dimensions: Pain Point Intensity, Audience Size, Competition Level (inverted), and Monetization Potential.

  • 1
    Score the idea (10 min)

    Rate all four dimensions. Pull search volume, check Etsy/Gumroad competition, estimate willingness to pay.

  • 2
    Run the MVP test

    For scores 12-14: ship a minimal version - single-page guide, stripped template, 3-video mini-course - in under a week.

  • 3
    Pre-sell before building

    For 15+ scores: use the presell checklist (headline, early bird price, scarcity, email capture). No buyers = no build.

  • 4
    Validate, then promote to Product Vault

    MVP sales or presell signups graduate the idea to Product Vault status "In Development." No validation = stays in backlog.

40-Point Launch Checklist

Never Miss a Launch Step Again

🏠 Phase 1 - Product Creation
Product fully built and tested (or MVP complete for presell)
All file formats confirmed and zipped for delivery
Cover image and mockups created (platform-specific sizes)
Welcome note / onboarding PDF prepared for buyers
FAQ document drafted based on likely questions
License terms and refund policy written
🖥 Phase 2 - Platform Setup
Product listed on all target platforms with early bird price
Delivery tested - buyer flow completed end-to-end
Description written with primary keywords + tags per platform
Upsell or bundle offer configured at checkout
Affiliate program enabled (Gumroad / Payhip)
Analytics tracking confirmed and firing
📄 Phase 3 - Sales Page & Email
Sales page headline tested - CTA visible above fold on mobile
Social proof section in place (even 1-2 early reviews)
Launch email written and scheduled to full list
3-email launch sequence (announce, value, last chance)
Post-purchase sequence set up in ESP
Review request email scheduled 7 days post-purchase
🚨 Phase 4 - Launch Day
Launch email sent to full list at optimal send time
Announcements live on all social channels + stories
Relevant communities notified (not spammed)
First 10 buyers personally thanked
Hourly sales monitoring active for 24 hours
Early buyers asked for feedback + screenshot reviews
📈 Phase 5 - Post-Launch Optimization
Revenue and units logged in Revenue Dashboard
Conversion rate calculated and noted for future reference
Reviews captured in Customer Feedback Tracker
Sales page updated with new social proof
Evergreen promo plan added to Marketing Calendar
Version 2.0 feedback notes filed in Idea Backlog
