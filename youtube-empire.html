FAQ

Common questions

Do I need a paid Notion account?

No. YouTube Empire works on Notion's free plan. All databases, views, and templates are fully compatible with free accounts. If you want to invite collaborators such as an editor, you'll need a paid Notion plan — but solo use is completely free.

What exactly do I get after purchase?

You receive an email with a Notion duplicate link. Click it while logged into Notion and the entire YouTube Empire workspace — all six databases with pre-built views, templates, and example data — is added to your account instantly. Customize it however you like from there.

Does it work on mobile?

Yes. Notion has iOS and Android apps and YouTube Empire works well on both. You can log ideas, check your content calendar, and update video statuses from your phone. For heavy database editing and setup, desktop is easier — but mobile is great for quick updates on the go.

I'm fairly new to Notion. Will I be able to figure this out?

Absolutely. YouTube Empire is designed to be immediately usable, not a Notion puzzle to solve. Every database has clear property names, pre-set filters, and one-click template buttons. If you've used Notion for basic notes, you can use this from day one — no prior database experience needed.

Do I get updates when new features are added?

Yes. Your purchase includes lifetime access and all future updates. When we add new views, templates, or databases to YouTube Empire, you receive a link to the updated version at no extra cost — forever.

What if I manage multiple YouTube channels?

You can duplicate the template as many times as you need — one copy per channel, or a single master workspace with each channel as a separate section. Many multi-channel creators use one workspace and use Notion's filtering to separate channels within shared databases like the pipeline and sponsorship tracker.
