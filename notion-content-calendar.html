3 Views to Set Up

The real power of a Notion content calendar comes from switching between views. Here are the three you need:

📊

Table View

Your default planning view. See all content in a sortable, filterable table. Sort by Publish Date, filter by Platform or Status to focus on what needs attention right now.

📅

Calendar View

See your publishing schedule as a calendar. Instantly spot gaps in your schedule and busy days at a glance. Set the date property to Publish Date and watch it auto-populate.

🖼️

Gallery View

Display content as visual cards using cover images or thumbnails. Great for reviewing finished content before publishing and for sharing your content plan with a team or client.
