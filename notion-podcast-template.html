Podcast SEO: Show Notes That Actually Rank

CreatorHQ Pro's show notes template is built for SEO from the ground up. Each template section maps to a known SEO best practice:

  • Episode title: Target keyword in title, episode number, and guest name if relevant
  • Episode summary (150 words): Natural keyword inclusion, answers "what will I learn from this episode"
  • Timestamps: Creates jump links that reduce bounce rate and signal rich content to Google
  • Key takeaways: Scannable bullet points that often appear in featured snippets
  • Full transcript: Thousands of naturally keyword-rich words per episode — the biggest SEO asset most podcasters ignore
  • Guest bio with links: Earns backlinks when guests share the episode page
