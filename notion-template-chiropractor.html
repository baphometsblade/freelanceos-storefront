Frequently Asked Questions

Questions From Chiropractors

Does this Notion template store patient records?

No. This template is strictly for business management — tracking new patient inquiries, revenue goals, referral sources, CE credits, and marketing tasks. All patient records and clinical documentation must remain in your HIPAA-compliant EHR or practice management software. Notion is not HIPAA-compliant and should never be used for PHI.

Can I track where my new patients come from?

Yes. The Referral Source Tracker lets you log every referral channel — primary care doctors, gyms, corporate wellness partners, social media, Google ads — and see which sources produce the most new inquiries so you can invest your marketing budget wisely. This is one of the most valuable features for cash-pay practice owners.

How does the new patient inquiry pipeline work?

The pipeline tracks the business side of new patient acquisition — lead source, inquiry date, consultation scheduled, and whether they converted to an active patient. No clinical information is stored, only business development data. It works like a simple CRM specifically designed for practice growth.

Can I use Notion to track my continuing education hours?

Absolutely. The CE and License Renewal Tracker logs every course you take, the credit hours earned, the topic, the provider, and the expiry date for your licenses. You can filter by state and specialty to stay compliant without scrambling at renewal time. This feature alone saves most practice owners significant stress each renewal cycle.

What does the revenue dashboard show?

The practice revenue dashboard tracks your monthly revenue goals vs. actuals, patient visit volume targets, cash-pay vs. insurance splits in aggregate (not per patient), and year-to-date progress. It gives you a clear financial pulse on your practice without being an accounting system — use it alongside your bookkeeper or accounting software.

Will this work for a cash-pay chiropractic practice?

This template is particularly well-suited to cash-pay and direct-access practices where the owner is also managing marketing, referral relationships, and business development directly. The lead tracking and referral source features were built with independent practice owners in mind. If you run a cash-pay model and want to grow through referrals and marketing, this OS gives you the infrastructure to do it systematically.
