FAQ
Common questions

How do I actually use these templates — do they integrate with Gmail?

The templates live in Notion as text. To use one: open it, fill in the variables, copy the body text, paste into Gmail (or any email client), add your subject line, send. It takes about 90 seconds. There's no direct Gmail integration, but the copy-paste workflow is faster than any plugin once you know where your templates are.

Are these templates written for specific freelance niches?

They're written for the universal freelance situations — invoicing, project delivery, client communication — that apply to designers, developers, writers, consultants, and any other service provider. The variables let you customize them instantly for your niche and client type.

What email templates are included in the free version?

The FreelanceOS Lite (free) version includes 5 basic templates: a generic follow-up, a project intro, and a simple invoice reminder. The Pro version includes all 30+ templates across 8 categories including the full invoice follow-up sequence, scope change communications, and the referral request system.

Can I add my own templates to the library?

Yes — and we encourage it. The Notion database is fully editable. Just create a new entry, add your template text, tag it with a category, and it shows up in your search results. Many users build custom templates for their specific industry within the first week.
