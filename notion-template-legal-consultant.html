Frequently Asked Questions

Questions From Legal Consultants

Is this a legal practice management system or case management software?

No. FreelanceOS Pro is a business management tool for legal professionals — not a dedicated legal practice management system, not case management software, and not a compliant legal document storage platform. For full practice management with court deadline tracking, document assembly, conflict checking automation, and trust accounting, evaluate dedicated legal practice management platforms (Clio, MyCase, PracticePanther, Smokeball, etc.). This template manages the business side of a legal consulting practice: client relationships, matter overview, time tracking, billing summaries, research notes, and practice financials — not case management or privileged document storage.

Can I use this to log billable hours and manage invoicing?

Yes — the Time and Billing Log is designed specifically for this. You can log each time entry with matter, task description, date, hours, billing rate, billable or non-billable classification, invoice number, and payment status. This gives you a running time log per matter that makes billing summaries fast to generate and helps you spot scope overruns early. For formal trust accounting and IOLTA compliance requirements, use your jurisdiction-approved accounting system — this template supplements your billing workflow but does not replace regulated trust accounting software or serve as an IOLTA-compliant ledger.

How do I use the contract review log without storing confidential contract content?

The Contract Review Log is designed to track your review workflow — not to store the actual contract document or its full text. Each entry captures matter and client name, contract type, key terms flagged for attention (described at a summary level you determine is appropriate), red lines or concerns identified, your recommendation (approve, approve with modifications, or decline), turnaround time, and the client's decision. The actual contract document and any privileged mark-up should be stored in your firm's secure document management system. This log tracks your process and business outputs, not privileged content — and what summary-level information you include is a professional judgment call you make in accordance with your bar obligations.

Is this appropriate for contract attorneys doing document review?

FreelanceOS Pro is better suited to independent legal consultants, outside general counsel, and attorneys running their own practice than to document review contractors working within a staffing agency's or law firm's managed review platform. If you are an independent legal consultant advising businesses on contracts, regulatory compliance, M&A due diligence, IP strategy, employment law matters, or data privacy — the matter tracker, time log, client CRM, research notes library, and practice dashboard are directly applicable to how you work. If you are a contract attorney primarily doing document review through a legal staffing agency, the agency's document review platform is the right tool for that specific workflow.
