What to Look for in Any Notion Template

Regardless of which template you choose, here are the quality markers that separate genuinely useful templates from pretty-looking ones:

  • Relational databases: Good templates use relations between databases, not just tags. Relations enable rollups and cross-database filtering.
  • Pre-built views: The template should come with the key filtered views already set up. You shouldn't have to build your "Today's Tasks" view from scratch.
  • Included templates: Notion templates inside the template — recurring page templates for weekly reviews, meeting notes, project kickoffs — are a sign of operational depth.
  • Quick-start documentation: A good template includes a setup guide so you're productive within the first hour, not spending days figuring out the intended workflow.
  • Works on free Notion plan: You shouldn't need a paid Notion subscription to use a paid template. All of our templates work on Notion's free plan.
