Frequently Asked Questions

Questions From Lawyers

How does the case tracker handle multiple concurrent cases?

The case tracker is a database with each case having its own record — client, matter type, status, key deadlines, linked documents, and billing information. You can view all open cases in a kanban or list view, filter by client or deadline, and see at a glance what needs attention today.

Can I track billable hours per case in Notion?

Yes. The billable hours log lets you record time entries per case — date, time spent, task description, and billable rate. Each case's total hours are calculated automatically so you always know what to invoice and have a record to support your bills.

Does this replace a legal practice management system?

FreelanceOS Pro is a business organization system, not a HIPAA or legal-compliance platform. It's ideal for organizing your work, tracking deadlines, and managing client relationships. For regulated document storage or trust accounting, you'll still need purpose-built legal software.

How does the conflict of interest checker work?

The conflict checker is a searchable client and matter database. Before taking on a new matter, you search by party name to surface any existing representations or adverse relationships. It's a systematic way to run the check every time — not rely on memory.
