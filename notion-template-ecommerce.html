Frequently Asked Questions

Questions From Ecommerce Sellers

Can I manage multiple ecommerce channels like Etsy and Shopify in one Notion workspace?

Yes. The product catalog database lets you tag products by channel — Etsy, Shopify, Amazon, wholesale — and filter by channel to see your full inventory per platform. You manage the business view centrally in Notion while each platform handles the actual transactions.

How does the supplier tracker work for product sourcing?

The supplier tracker is a database of all your suppliers — product category, minimum order quantity, lead time, payment terms, and contact info. Link each product to its supplier so you always know who to reorder from and how long it takes, before you run out.

Does the template include a marketing calendar for product launches?

Yes. The marketing calendar lets you plan promotional content, email campaigns, social posts, and launch dates per product. You can see all planned marketing across channels in one view and make sure launches are properly supported before they go live.

Can I use this for a digital product store as well as physical products?

Yes. FreelanceOS Pro works for physical and digital product businesses. Digital sellers typically use it for product catalog management, launch planning, and financial tracking. Physical sellers also use the supplier tracker and inventory alert system. The template adapts to either business model.
