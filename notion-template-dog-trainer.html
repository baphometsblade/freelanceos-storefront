Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be a Notion expert to use this template?
No. FreelanceOS Pro is built for non-technical users. After duplicating the template to your Notion account, everything is pre-built and labeled. You just start filling in your clients and sessions. Most dog trainers are up and running within an hour.
Can I use this for both private sessions and group classes?
Yes. FreelanceOS Pro includes both a Session Tracker for individual clients and a Group Class Roster for managing class-based formats. Both are included in the $29 one-time purchase, and you can use whichever components fit your business.
Is this a HIPAA or GDPR-compliant client management system?
FreelanceOS Pro is built on Notion and is not a HIPAA or legally-compliant data management system. For dog trainers storing sensitive personal data, use appropriate privacy settings in Notion and consult applicable data privacy requirements in your region. Notion's privacy settings allow you to keep your workspace private.
Can I customize the training program templates for my methods?
Absolutely. The Training Program Library is fully editable. You can add, remove, or rewrite every program to match your methods — whether you use positive reinforcement, marker training, sport dog training, or any other approach. It's your workspace.
Does this work on mobile so I can update session notes at the facility?
Yes. Notion works on iPhone and Android. You can open your Session Tracker on your phone at the facility and log notes immediately after each session while the details are fresh. The mobile app is free and syncs instantly with your desktop workspace.
