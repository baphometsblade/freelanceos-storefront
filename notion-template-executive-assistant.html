Can I use this to support multiple principals or clients at the same time?

Yes — supporting multiple principals simultaneously is one of the primary use cases this template is designed for. The Principal and Client Tracker gives you a complete profile for each executive or entrepreneur you support, including their preferences, priorities, timezone, and communication channels. The Task Priority Queue is linked to each principal so you can view all tasks across all clients in one master queue and filter or sort by principal, due date, or priority level at any point during your workday.

How does the task queue work when I'm supporting high-volume inbox management?

The Task and Priority Queue is your central dispatch system for everything incoming across all principals. Each task entry captures the principal it belongs to, task category (admin, research, communications, scheduling, travel, project support, or personal errands), priority level (urgent, high, medium, low), due date, status (backlog, in progress, blocked, done, or delegated), and any relevant notes. For EAs managing high-volume inboxes and fast-moving founders, filtering the queue by principal and priority makes it instantly clear what needs attention first — removing the mental overhead of multi-client task juggling.

Does this work for virtual or remote executive assistants?

Yes — FreelanceOS Pro is particularly well suited to remote and virtual executive assistants. The entire workspace lives in Notion and is accessible from anywhere. The Principal Tracker captures timezone and preferred communication channel (email, Slack, Voxer, text, phone, WhatsApp) for each client so you are always aligned on how they want to be reached and when. The Vendor and Contact Database helps remote EAs maintain a complete directory of recurring contacts without relying on a physical office's institutional knowledge.

Can I track travel bookings and itineraries with this template?

Yes.
