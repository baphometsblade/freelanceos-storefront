The Problem & The Fix
Freelance finances are uniquely messy
Pain Point

"Tax season hits and I have no idea what I owe — it's always a panic."

Freelancers often hit Q4 with no tax savings, leading to stress, payment plans, and penalties. Most people only realize the problem when it's too late.

The Fix

The automatic tax reserve calculator shows your estimated liability every month. Set aside 30% and watch the number grow alongside your income — no April surprises.

Pain Point

"I make good money but I'm never sure why my bank account doesn't reflect it."

Without visibility into expenses, software subscriptions and small purchases accumulate invisibly and erode your margins without you noticing.

The Fix

The expense dashboard categorizes every outgoing dollar. You'll immediately see that $312/month in software or the $85 ad spend that's delivering nothing — and you can cut accordingly.

Pain Point

"I'm scared to raise my rates because I don't know if I'm actually profitable."

Without a clear profit margin view, rate decisions are guesswork. You might be undercharging by 40% and not know it.

The Fix

The monthly P&L shows your real profit margin by client and project type. Once you see that Client A generates 95% margin and Client B generates 60%, the rate conversation becomes obvious.

FAQ
Common questions

Is this a substitute for accounting software?

For most freelancers who are sole proprietors or single-member LLCs earning under $150k/year, this Notion template captures everything you need for tax prep and monthly financial clarity. It won't replace QuickBooks for complex businesses with employees or inventory — but it's ideal for independent consultants, designers, writers, and developers.

Can I track multiple income currencies?

Yes. You can add a currency field to each income entry and filter/group by currency. The dashboard totals work in any currency — just use your preferred one consistently as the base.

Does the budget template work for new freelancers with irregular income?

Especially well. The monthly snapshot view is designed for variable income — it shows you your actual numbers without assuming a fixed salary. The zero-month view (when income is low) is just as informative as the good months, helping you plan your emergency runway.

Is this template included in the free version of FreelanceOS?

The Budget Template is a Pro feature. The free version of FreelanceOS includes a stripped-down income log, but the full budget dashboard with expense categories, tax calculator, annual P&L, and client-linked income is exclusive to FreelanceOS Pro.
