How It Works

Capture. Organize. Retrieve.

Three steps. No complicated setup. Your notes become useful the moment you duplicate the template.

1

Capture with Structure

Open a pre-built note template for the exact situation you're in — a client call just ended, a project briefing just happened, or you have a research insight worth keeping. Each template prompts you for exactly what matters: context, decisions, action items, and follow-up. You're not staring at a blank page. You're answering clear questions.
