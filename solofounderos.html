What's Inside

8 modules. One connected operating system for your whole business.

Every module is pre-built with views, templates, and example data. Start using it on day one — no setup wizard required.

Module 01
💡

Idea Vault & Validation Framework

Capture, score, and validate every product idea before you spend a single hour building. Includes a 5-criteria scoring matrix, market size estimator, and a "Kill or Keep" decision log so you stop working on the wrong things.

Module 02
🗺️

Product Roadmap (Now / Next / Later)

A clean, opinionated roadmap framework inspired by how the best indie teams ship fast. Drop features into Now, Next, or Later with linked user stories, effort estimates, and impact scores. Kill scope creep before it starts.

Module 03
📈

Revenue Dashboard & MRR Tracker

Log every revenue event — new sales, refunds, upgrades, churn — and watch your MRR, ARR, and LTV calculate automatically. Weekly and monthly views let you spot momentum shifts before they become problems.

Module 04
🔬

Customer Research Hub

Never lose a user interview again. Store feedback, tag by theme, link insights directly to roadmap items, and maintain a living document of your ICP. Know who you're building for — and build only for them.

Module 05
🚀

Launch Command Center

A 4-week pre-launch checklist covering product, marketing, distribution, and ops. Includes a Product Hunt launch day timeline, email sequence planner, press kit template, and post-launch retrospective framework.

Module 06
🔍

Weekly Founder Review

A 15-minute weekly cadence template that tracks wins, blockers, top priority for next week, energy level, and one thing to eliminate. Monthly rollups surface patterns so you can see exactly where your time is going.

Module 07
🕵️

Competitor Intelligence Board

Monitor up to 20 competitors across pricing, positioning, feature set, and messaging. Get alerts when a competitor launches something new. Never be the last to know about a market shift again.

Module 08
📣

Build-in-Public Content Planner

A full content calendar for Twitter/X, LinkedIn, and blog — pre-loaded with 30 build-in-public post frameworks. Track engagement, link posts to milestones, and maintain a "content ideas" backlog so you never stare at a blank screen again.
