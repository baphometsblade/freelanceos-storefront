The Problem

Managing Investments Without a System Is Costing You Clarity

Sound familiar? Most investors have accounts spread across five platforms and still can't answer a basic question: what is my net worth right now?

🗂️

No Central View of All Your Investments

Your stocks are in Fidelity, your crypto is on Coinbase, your rental property is in a spreadsheet, and your retirement account is somewhere else entirely. There's no single place that shows you the full picture of your financial life, and every time you want to assess your situation you have to log into five different apps.

🧮

Manually Calculating Returns Is Exhausting

Figuring out your total return, average cost basis, and gain/loss percentage requires digging through transaction histories and building messy spreadsheet formulas that break whenever you add a new position. You end up doing hours of work just to see a number that should take seconds to pull up.

💸

No System to Track Dividend Income

Dividend investors often have no idea what their total annual income is or when the next payment is coming. Without a dividend log, you can't plan reinvestment strategies, project income growth, or know what your effective yield on cost actually is across your whole portfolio.

🌫️

Unclear Net Worth Picture

Net worth is assets minus liabilities, but who actually tracks both in a single place? Most people guess their net worth instead of knowing it. When you don't track it monthly, you can't see trends, celebrate milestones, or spot problems before they become serious financial setbacks.

FAQ

Everything You Want to Know

Honest answers to the most common questions about the Notion investment tracker template.

Does the Notion investment tracker connect to brokerage accounts automatically? +
No, and that's by design. The template does not connect to your brokerage accounts, banks, or crypto exchanges via API. It is a fully manual tracking system where you enter your holdings, prices, and transactions yourself. This keeps your financial data entirely within your private Notion workspace — no third-party service ever touches your account credentials or balances. Many investors prefer this approach for privacy and full control. You update prices as often as you want, which takes just a few minutes per week.
Can I track cryptocurrency with this Notion portfolio tracker? +
Yes, absolutely. The Crypto & Alternative Assets module lets you log any cryptocurrency — Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana, or any altcoin — by ticker symbol, quantity held, purchase price per coin, and current value. The module also supports NFTs, precious metals like gold and silver, collectibles, and private equity holdings. Your crypto positions are rolled up into the Portfolio Overview so you see their percentage of your overall allocation alongside your stocks and other assets.
Is this Notion investment tracker template suitable for beginners? +
Yes, it's built to serve investors at every experience level. If you're just starting out, you can focus entirely on the Portfolio Overview Dashboard and the Net Worth Calculator — those two modules alone give you immediate clarity that most beginners never have. As you grow more comfortable, the Stock & ETF Tracker, Dividend Income Log, and Investment Goals Tracker are all pre-built and waiting. Advanced investors will appreciate the Tax & Records Hub and the detailed cost basis tracking. You don't need any Notion experience beyond knowing how to click a duplicate link.
Does the template calculate my FIRE number? +
Yes. The Investment Goals Tracker module includes a built-in FIRE calculator based on the 25x annual expenses rule (equivalent to a 4% safe withdrawal rate). You enter your estimated annual expenses in retirement, and the template calculates your FIRE number automatically. You can also set a target date and track your progress as a percentage of your goal each month. The module includes Coast FIRE and Lean FIRE variants so you can see multiple paths to financial independence simultaneously.
Can I track real estate investments in the Notion portfolio tracker? +
Yes. The Real Estate Investment Tracker module is specifically built for rental property investors and REIT holders. For physical properties, you log the purchase price, current estimated market value, outstanding mortgage balance, monthly rental income, and recurring expenses (property taxes, insurance, maintenance). Notion formulas auto-calculate your net equity, cash-on-cash return, cap rate, and appreciation since purchase. REIT holdings are also tracked via the main Stock & ETF Tracker with a "REIT" tag for filtering.
Is my financial data secure if I use Notion to track investments? +
Your data lives exclusively in your private Notion workspace, protected by Notion's enterprise-grade AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS in transit. You control all sharing permissions — the template is set to private by default and is never shared with anyone unless you explicitly choose to. Because the template uses no external API connections to your accounts, your brokerage credentials, account numbers, and real balances are never transmitted anywhere. You can also export your Notion pages locally as a backup at any time through Notion's export feature.
