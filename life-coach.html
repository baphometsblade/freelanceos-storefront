FAQ

Questions from coaches and consultants specifically

Can I store session notes and client progress inside the system?

Yes. The Client CRM has a dedicated notes section per client where you can store session summaries, key breakthroughs, action items from each call, and ongoing observations. Since it's Notion, you can use headers, bullet lists, dates, and tags to organize notes exactly how your practice works. Coaches typically create one page per client with sub-sections per session.

Does it handle both coaching packages and per-session billing?

Yes. The Invoice Generator supports upfront package billing (pay for 6 sessions, get access), monthly installment billing for packages, and per-session billing. Each client in your CRM shows their active package, sessions used, sessions remaining, and billing status — so you always know where each client stands financially.

Is this appropriate for business/executive consultants, not just life coaches?

Yes — and roughly 40% of buyers in this category are business strategy, leadership, and executive consultants rather than life coaches. The modules (CRM, goal tracking, proposal builder, finance dashboard) apply equally to any high-touch, session-based consulting practice. The system uses the word "client" generically and all labels are customizable to your terminology.

I already use a scheduling tool (Calendly, Acuity). Will this overlap?

No overlap — FreelanceOS Pro is your business operations layer, not your booking layer. Calendly/Acuity handles client self-booking. FreelanceOS handles everything that comes after: session notes, goal progress, invoicing, and practice financials. Most coaches run both side by side — scheduling tool for client-facing booking, FreelanceOS for internal business management.
