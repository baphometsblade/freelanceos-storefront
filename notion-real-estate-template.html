What's Inside

8 Complete Modules — One Unified Workspace

Every module is linked together in Notion's relational database system. Your properties connect to your tenants, which connect to your income records, which roll up into your portfolio overview. This is the database architecture that expensive real estate software charges hundreds per month for — built inside your free Notion account.

Module 01

Property Database

Central hub for every property you own, rent, or are evaluating. Fields include address, property type, square footage, bed/bath count, year built, purchase price, current value, mortgage details, status, photo attachments, and linked notes. Filter by status, location, or property type instantly.

Module 02

Deal Pipeline

Track acquisition opportunities through every stage: Prospect → Due Diligence → Offer Submitted → Under Contract → Closed (or Dead). Each deal card links to the property, stores your analysis notes, and shows projected vs. actual numbers.

Module 03

Rental Income & Expense Tracker

Log every rent payment, late fee, repair cost, insurance premium, property tax, and management fee against a specific property. Rollup formulas calculate monthly net income automatically — clean records ready for your accountant at tax time.

Module 04

Tenant CRM

Full contact records for every tenant including name, phone, email, employer, lease start and end dates, monthly rent, security deposit amount, payment history log, and notes. Get notified when leases are approaching renewal.

Module 05

Maintenance Request Log

Track every repair and maintenance task: date reported, property, unit, issue description, priority (urgent / routine / cosmetic), vendor assigned, cost, and resolution date. Filter open issues by property and priority so nothing falls through the cracks.

Module 06

Market Research Notes

Store comparable sales data, neighborhood research, school ratings, walkability scores, local rent trends, and notes on target submarkets. Build a searchable research library that informs every acquisition decision you make.

Module 07

ROI Calculator

Input purchase price, down payment, gross rents, vacancy rate, operating expenses, and financing costs. Formula fields instantly calculate cash-on-cash return, cap rate, gross rent multiplier, and monthly cash flow — the four numbers every investor needs before making an offer.

Module 08

Document Vault

Organized storage for lease agreements, inspection reports, closing documents, insurance policies, warranties, contractor invoices, and HOA documents. Linked to the relevant property so you can pull up any document in seconds.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions About the Notion Real Estate Template

Can I track multiple properties in this Notion template?
Yes — the Property Database holds unlimited properties, each with its own page: address, specs, photos, financials, status, linked tenants, and deals. Filter and sort by property type, status, location, or ROI to see your whole portfolio at a glance.
How do I calculate ROI in Notion for a rental property?
The ROI Calculator has fields for purchase price, down payment, rent, mortgage, insurance, taxes, repairs, vacancy rate, and management fees. Notion formula fields automatically calculate cash-on-cash return and cap rate. You enter the numbers — the template does the math.
Can I share this Notion template with a partner or spouse?
Yes. Invite your partner, co-investor, or property manager as a Notion member with view or edit access. Both see the same live data — no emailing spreadsheets back and forth. Ideal for house hunting couples, co-investors, or landlords splitting management duties.
Is this Notion real estate template for residential or commercial properties?
Primarily residential — single-family homes, condos, townhomes, small multifamily (2–4 units), and vacation rentals. Many users adapt it for small commercial properties too. All fields and dropdowns are fully customizable to match what you own or invest in.
Can I use this Notion template if I'm just house hunting (not an investor)?
Absolutely. The house hunting section includes a property comparison database, scoring rubric, must-have checklist, neighborhood notes, showing log, and offer comparison tracker. Many first-time buyers use it to evaluate 10–30+ homes systematically before making an offer — the rest of the template stays hidden until you need it.
How do I track rental income vs. expenses in Notion?
The Rental Income and Expense Tracker lets you log every payment and expense against a specific property. Rollup formulas calculate monthly and annual net income automatically. At tax time, filter by property and date range for a clean Schedule E-ready summary your accountant will appreciate.
