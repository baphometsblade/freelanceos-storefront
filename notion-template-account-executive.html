Frequently Asked Questions

Is this Notion template built for account executives specifically — not account managers?
Yes. This template is built for quota-carrying salespeople: pipeline management, prospecting, closing, and quota attainment tracking. It is not designed for account management, which focuses on relationship maintenance and retention. If you carry a number and close new business — enterprise AE, mid-market AE, inside sales, SMB sales, or freelance sales consultant — this is your template.
Can I use this as a lightweight CRM to replace Salesforce for my personal pipeline?
Yes. The Deal Pipeline database is designed as a personal pipeline CRM — deals with stage, size, expected close date, weighted value, champion status, competition, and next action. Many AEs use this alongside Salesforce (for CRM compliance with their team) and Notion (for actual prep and thinking). It gives you pipeline visibility without the Salesforce UX tax.
How does the account research notes section work?
The Account Research Notes database holds one entry per target account — company overview, ICP fit score, tech stack identified from LinkedIn or BuiltWith, trigger events like funding rounds or hiring surges, decision-makers identified with LinkedIn profile notes, buying process, budget cycle, and existing competitor contracts. Before any important call, you pull up the account page to review everything you know in one place.
Can I track commission and comp plan attainment?
Yes. The Commission Tracker logs each closed deal with deal size, commission rate, commission amount, any accelerators, OTE attainment percentage, expected payout date, and notes. This is especially useful for freelance sales consultants on commission-only arrangements and for AEs in complex comp plans who want to verify their earnings before payroll deposits.
