Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a clinical health management or patient record system?
No. FreelanceOS Pro is a business management and session organization tool for wellness coaches. It is not a clinical documentation system, EMR, EHR, or HIPAA-compliant platform. Never use this template to store protected health information or clinical records. Wellness coaches are not licensed healthcare providers, and this template is designed for organizing your coaching practice — not for clinical treatment planning. Always refer clients with health conditions to licensed medical professionals.
Can I manage multiple wellness clients and programs at once?
Yes. The Client Wellness Journey Tracker shows all active clients with their program, current week, habit completion rate, and next session date in a single dashboard view. You can filter by program type, wellness goal, or program week to quickly see where each client stands across your full roster — whether you have 5 clients or 25.
How does the session notes system work?
The Session Notes template is designed for coaching-oriented notes — not clinical records. Each entry captures the session date, focus area, key insights and breakthroughs, the habit or practice assigned, the client's accountability commitment for next week, and the next session focus. You pull up the previous session note before each call so you arrive fully prepared and context-aware without relying on memory.
Can I build recurring wellness programs and deliver them to multiple clients?
Yes. The Program Curriculum Library lets you build programs once — including weekly themes, protocols, assignments, and worksheets — then use them with multiple clients. Each client's record tracks which week of the program they are on, so you can deliver consistently without rebuilding the curriculum for each new enrollment. Your programs become a reusable business asset.

One-time purchase of $29. You get lifetime access to the template and all future updates with no recurring fees. No monthly subscription, no per-client fees, and no annual renewal required.
