FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before applying.

When do I get paid? +
Payouts are processed monthly via Gumroad. Once your balance reaches the minimum threshold, Gumroad sends your earnings automatically to your PayPal or bank account (depending on your Gumroad payout settings). There is no action needed on your end after setup.
Is there a minimum payout amount? +
Gumroad's minimum payout threshold is $10. Any balance below this rolls over to the next month and accumulates until it crosses the threshold. Most affiliates hit their first payout within their first 2–3 referrals.
How long does the cookie last? +
Your affiliate link has a 60-day cookie window. This means if someone clicks your link on day 1 and purchases on day 58, you still get the full 30% commission. This is one of the longest cookie windows in the Notion template space, giving you maximum earning potential even from content published months ago.
How does tracking work? +
Gumroad handles all tracking through your unique affiliate URL. When someone clicks your link, a cookie is set in their browser. Any purchase from the FreelanceOS product catalog within 60 days is attributed to your link and you earn the commission. You can see real-time stats — clicks, conversions, and earnings — in your Gumroad affiliate dashboard.
What if a customer asks for a refund? +
We offer a 30-day no-questions-asked refund policy to customers. In the event of a refund, the corresponding commission is reversed. Refund rates on our products are consistently below 2%, so this is rarely an issue in practice.
Do I need a website or a big audience? +
No minimum follower count is required. We care about audience fit more than audience size. A niche newsletter with 500 subscribers who are all freelancers will outperform a general blog with 50,000 visitors. Tell us about your audience in your application and we will make a decision based on that context.
Can I promote all products or just one? +
Once approved, you receive individual affiliate links for every product in the catalog — FreelanceOS Pro, CreatorHQ Pro, AgencyOS Pro, the Bundle, and YouTube Empire. You can promote as many or as few as suit your audience. Promoting the higher-priced YouTube Empire product ($97) earns you $29.10 per sale, which is one of the highest per-sale commissions available for a Notion template product.
