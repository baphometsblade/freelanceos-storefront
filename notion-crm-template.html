What a Notion CRM Can Do

Dedicated CRM tools like HubSpot and Pipedrive are powerful but overkill for most freelancers. A well-built Notion CRM gives you 80% of the capability at zero cost — and it lives right alongside your projects and invoices.

📋

Pipeline View

See every prospect and client as a Kanban card moving through stages: Lead → Contacted → Proposal Sent → Client → Archived. Drag and drop to update status instantly.

📚

Full Client History

Every interaction, project, invoice, and note lives on the client page. Open any contact and see the complete relationship history at a glance — no hunting through emails.

📌

Task Linking

Link client records to your task database so every action item — send proposal, follow up, kick off project — is tied to the right client and shows up in your task view.

📧

Email Logging

Paste email summaries directly into the client Notion page. Build a searchable log of every important conversation without paying for CRM software.
