Frequently Asked Questions

Questions From Architects

Can I track multiple architectural projects simultaneously?

Yes. The Project Phase Tracker gives each project its own record with phases (schematic design, design development, construction documents, bidding, and construction administration), completion percentages, and linked deliverables. Filter by phase or status to see your full workload at a glance.

How does the client approval log work?

The Client Approval Log tracks every submittal requiring client sign-off — drawings, material selections, change orders, and design revisions. Each entry records the submission date, client response deadline, approval date, and notes so nothing falls through the cracks.

Does it include a material specification database?

Yes. The Specification Database stores material specs with manufacturer, product code, finish, lead time, cost, and the project it belongs to. Link specs to design phases and use the database to generate coordinated material schedules.

Can I manage multiple contractors on a single project?

Yes. The Contractor Hub logs each subcontractor with contact info, scope of work, contract amount, schedule milestones, and RFI status. Filter by project to see all contractors working on a specific building at once.

How does billing milestone tracking work?

The Invoice Milestones database ties each invoice to a project phase and deliverable. Log the milestone description, invoice amount, invoice date, and payment received date. Rollups show total billed vs. collected per project so you always know your financial position.
