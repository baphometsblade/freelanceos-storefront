Frequently Asked Questions

Does this help me track my ISA Certified Arborist CEU requirements?
Yes. The Certification & Professional Development Log tracks your ISA Certified Arborist number, expiration date, CEUs required per 3-year renewal cycle (300 CEUs), CEUs completed this cycle, and upcoming CEU sources — ISA events, TCIA events, university extensions, and state arborist association conferences. It also covers TRAQ, BCMA, state pesticide applicator license, and safety certifications including OSHA 10/30 and aerial rescue training.
Can I track both tree removal and plant health care programs?
Yes. FreelanceOS Pro includes a Work Order & Job Log covering all pruning types, removal, stump grinding, cabling, and emergency storm work. A separate Plant Health Care Program Tracker covers PHC clients on ongoing treatment programs, with tree species, pest or disease issue, treatment approach categories, and follow-up scheduling tracked per client. Note that detailed pesticide application records required by your state applicator license must be maintained in compliant record-keeping systems.
Can I manage multiple crew members and equipment with this template?
Yes. The Work Order & Job Log records crew size and equipment deployed per job. The Equipment Fleet & Maintenance Tracker covers your entire fleet with service intervals, operating hours, inspection dates, and insurance renewal tracking. A Crew Scheduling & Safety Record section handles team coordination, and the Subcontractor Network section tracks crane operators and other specialty contractors you call in for larger jobs.
Does this track tree removal permits?
Yes. The client property assessment log includes a permit required field for flagging jobs that need municipal approval. A dedicated Permit Tracking Log section lets you track each protected tree removal permit application through the approval process, including jurisdiction, submission date, and status — useful when you're managing multiple permits across different municipalities simultaneously.
