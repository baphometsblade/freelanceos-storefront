Frequently Asked Questions

Is this Notion template built specifically for fundraising consultants and nonprofit development professionals?
Yes — every database is designed around the fundraising consultant's workflow: managing multiple client organizations and their campaigns, tracking donor prospect research and cultivation, monitoring grant deadlines and funder relationships, running event fundraising logistics, and operating the business side of a development consulting practice. It works for major gifts, annual fund, capital campaigns, planned giving, grant prospecting, special events, corporate partnerships, and interim development director engagements.
How does the grant deadline tracker work?
The Grant Deadline & Funder Tracker is the most critical operational tool for development professionals. It tracks funder name, funder type, grant program name, LOI and proposal deadlines, submission dates, award decisions, amounts received, and reporting deadlines for each grant cycle. Most grants require interim and final reports — missing a reporting deadline can jeopardize future funding. Filter by client organization or deadline date to see exactly what's due in the next 30-90 days across all your client organizations.
Can I manage fundraising campaigns for multiple nonprofit client organizations simultaneously?
Yes. The Client Organization & Campaign Tracker, Grant Deadline Tracker, and Major Gifts Portfolio all include a client organization field — you can manage campaigns, grant deadlines, and donor cultivation pipelines across multiple nonprofit clients at once. Filter by client to see everything relevant to a single organization without losing visibility into your other engagements.
How does the major gifts pipeline work, and is donor personal information stored?
The Major Gifts Portfolio is an administrative pipeline tracker — it uses prospect IDs and general stage tracking (identification/qualification/cultivation/solicitation/stewardship) rather than detailed personal donor information. Detailed donor profiles, giving history, and sensitive cultivation notes belong in your client's donor database (Bloomerang/Little Green Light/Salesforce NPSP/DonorPerfect/Raiser's Edge). This tracker ensures no prospect falls out of the pipeline during a complex 18-36 month cultivation process.
