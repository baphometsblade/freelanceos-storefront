Frequently Asked Questions

Questions From Nonprofit Leaders

Can I track multiple grants in this Notion template?

Yes. The Grant Tracker database lets you log every grant with funder name, award amount, application deadline, reporting deadline, and status. You can filter by funder, by program area, or by stage so you always know what's due and what's been awarded.

How does volunteer management work in Notion?

The Volunteer Management database stores each volunteer's contact info, skills, availability, and hours logged. You can assign volunteers to events and programs, track their total contribution hours, and send thank-you notes when they hit milestones.

Does the template include a donor CRM?

Yes. The Donor CRM tracks individual and organizational donors with donation history, communication log, and relationship notes. Filter by donation size, by program area, or by last contact date to prioritize your stewardship efforts.

Can I use this for board meeting preparation?

Yes. The Board Meeting Notes database provides a structured format for agendas, attendance records, motion logs, and action items. Previous meeting notes are searchable and linked to relevant programs and financial reports.

Is there an impact reporting tool included?

Yes. The Impact Reporting section helps you capture program outcomes, beneficiary numbers, and key metrics in a structured format. Export the data when writing grant reports or creating your annual report — no more starting from scratch each time.

How does budget tracking work in this template?

The Budget Tracker lets you log income sources (grants, donations, earned revenue) and expenses by program and overhead category. Rollup formulas calculate totals per program and compare actuals against budget, giving you a real-time view of organizational finances.

Can multiple staff members use this at the same time?

Yes. The template is designed for team collaboration. Share the Notion workspace with staff, board members, or key volunteers at different permission levels. Each person only needs a free Notion account to access their portion of the workspace.
