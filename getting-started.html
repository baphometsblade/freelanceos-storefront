Pro Tips for Your First Week

Save time and get more out of FreelanceOS faster
1

Start with just ONE database this week — don't try to fill everything at once. Add data as it comes in naturally.

2

Add your first client before you add any features — the CRM is the heart of the system. Real data makes everything click.

3

The Rate Calculator is the most-used feature — run your numbers today to see your true minimum rate.

4

Use the templates inside each database for recurring tasks — they save serious time on client onboarding and project kickoffs.

5

Check in with the dashboard every Monday for a weekly review — 10 minutes keeps the whole system healthy.
