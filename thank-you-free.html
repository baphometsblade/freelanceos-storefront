Quick Answers

Do I need a paid Notion account?

No. The Client Tracker works with a free Notion account. All templates in both the free and Pro version are compatible with Notion’s free plan.

What format are the templates?

They are native Notion templates. When you click the link we send you, you’ll see a “Duplicate” button in Notion — click it and the template is instantly copied to your workspace.

Can I share with my team?

Yes. Once duplicated to your Notion workspace, you can share it with teammates just like any other Notion page. Notion’s free plan supports up to 10 guests.
