FAQ

Questions from designers specifically

Can I actually track revision rounds per project?

Yes — that's one of the most-used features among designer buyers. Each project in the Project Manager has a revision counter you manually update each time a client requests changes. You can also add a notes field linking back to your contract's revision terms. It gives you a clear, timestamped record if a scope dispute ever arises.

Does it work for both brand/graphic designers and UX designers?

Yes. The system is flexible enough for both. Graphic designers tend to use the proposal builder and contract vault most. UX/product designers tend to rely more on the project manager and client CRM. You'll customize the deliverable names to match your workflow — the structure works for any design discipline.

I already have contracts in Google Docs. Do I have to start over?

No. The Contract Vault is a tracking layer — you store the document link, the signed status, renewal date, and key terms summary. Your actual contract file can stay in Google Drive, DocuSign, or wherever it lives. FreelanceOS just gives you a single place to see the status of all your contracts at a glance.
