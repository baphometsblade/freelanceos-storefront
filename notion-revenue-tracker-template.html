⬡ Features

Everything Your Revenue Dashboard Needs

Six core views built into the Notion revenue tracker, all connected through linked databases.

📅

Monthly Revenue Rollup

Every invoice you mark as collected rolls up to the correct month automatically. See invoiced vs. collected vs. outstanding for any month in seconds.

🎯

Annual Goal Tracker with Progress Bar

Set your annual income target and watch the progress bar move as revenue comes in. Know your pace, your gap, and what you need per month to hit your number.

🏆

Per-Client Revenue Ranking

A ranked view of every client by total revenue — filterable by month, quarter, or year. Instantly see who your most valuable clients are and who's underperforming.

🔮

Pipeline Value (Proposals Sent)

Track proposals you've sent that haven't been accepted yet. Pipeline value is kept separate from confirmed revenue so your numbers stay accurate and your forecast stays honest.

⚠️

Outstanding Invoice Tracker

See every unpaid invoice at a glance — how much is overdue, which clients owe you, and how long each invoice has been outstanding. Never forget to follow up again.

📈

MRR Trend View

Month-over-month revenue trends show you whether you're growing, plateauing, or sliding — before it becomes a problem. Spot patterns early and adjust your pipeline accordingly.
