Frequently Asked Questions

Is this Notion template designed specifically for tattoo artists?
Yes — every database is built around the tattoo artist's business cycle: managing client relationships and design history, tracking bookings and deposits, organizing design references by style, managing custom commissions, and tracking income. It works for fine line, traditional, realism, watercolor, geometric, and all other tattoo styles.
Does this replace formal client consent and health records?
No. FreelanceOS Pro is a business management tool, not a compliant health recordkeeping system. You must maintain required client consent forms and health records according to your local health authority and studio requirements using appropriate systems. This template handles business operations — client contact details, booking logistics, design references, income tracking — not formal health documentation or regulated clinical records.
How does the booking tracker work for managing a waitlist?
The Booking Tracker logs every inquiry and booking with client name, design brief, placement, size estimate, estimated hours, deposit paid, appointment date, session status, and total paid. You can use it as a waitlist manager by adding inquiries without an appointment date yet and assigning dates as slots open. This gives a clear view of your booking pipeline without relying on DMs, emails, and memory across multiple platforms.
Can I track both custom commissions and flash designs?
Yes. The Custom Design Tracker manages every commission from design stage through client approval and session booked. Separately, the Flash and Portfolio Tracker logs your flash designs with design name, price, availability status, and whether it's been posted on social media. Knowing which flash pieces are available and which have sold helps you plan content that drives inquiries and new bookings consistently.
