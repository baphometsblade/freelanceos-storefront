What's Inside

Six Modules Built for Brand & Commercial Photographers

📷

Client Shoot Tracker

Client name, brand/business type (personal brand, small business, e-commerce, food & beverage, corporate, product, lifestyle), shoot type, shoot date, location, status from inquiry through delivered through licensed, package booked, invoiced, paid. Every active client visible at a glance.

🎨

Brand Style Guide Database

Per client: brand colors with hex codes, typography direction, brand adjectives (bold, playful, minimal, luxe, earthy, approachable), reference image links, wardrobe direction, props direction, background preferences, what to avoid. Walk into every shoot fully briefed without rereading email chains.

📅

Shoot Planning & Shot List

Per shoot: shot list by category (hero images, secondary, detail/flat lay, lifestyle, portrait-only, video b-roll), equipment checklist, location notes, talent and styling direction, props list with sources, day-of timeline. The shoot brief that prevents missed shots and on-set chaos.

🖼

Gallery Delivery & Licensing Log

Per shoot: total images shot, finals delivered, editing preset used, delivery platform (Pixieset, Cloudspot, Google Drive), delivery date, client approval, licensed deliverables, usage rights granted, exclusivity period. Delivery status visible in seconds across every active project.

⚖

Commercial Licensing Tracker

Track per project: usage type (website, social, digital ads, print, billboard, broadcast), territory (local, national, international), duration, exclusivity, licensing fee charged, and expiry/renewal reminders. Your commercial licensing revenue stream tracked properly — often worth more than the shoot itself.

💼

Photography Studio Dashboard

Active clients, monthly revenue, income by shoot type, average session value, new inquiry conversion rate, referral source breakdown (Instagram, website SEO, past client referral, styled shoot exposure), gallery delivery turnaround average, and annual revenue goal — the full picture in one view.
