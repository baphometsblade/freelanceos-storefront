What exactly does the FreelanceOS Notion project management template include?
▾
FreelanceOS Pro includes 9 fully linked Notion databases: a Project Tracker with a 5-stage status pipeline, Client CRM, Task Board (Kanban + list + timeline views), Time Log with automatic hourly rate calculation, Invoice & Payment Tracker, Proposal Library, Deadline Calendar, Goals & Revenue Tracker, and a Weekly Dashboard that aggregates data from all other databases. Every database is pre-wired to work together — no manual linking or formula setup required. You also get a Quick Start guide and a pre-populated demo workspace so you can see exactly how everything connects before you start adding your own data.
Can I really track multiple client projects at the same time?
▾
Yes — multi-client project management is the core use case FreelanceOS was designed for. The Project Tracker supports unlimited projects linked to unlimited clients. You can filter by client, deadline, status, or revenue tier instantly. The Deadline Calendar pulls from every active project automatically so you always see your full workload in one view. If you have 8 clients and 12 active projects running simultaneously, FreelanceOS can handle it. There is no limit on the number of projects or clients you can add.
Does this Notion project management template require a paid Notion subscription?
▾
No. FreelanceOS Pro is built entirely on database features, formulas, linked views, and filters that are available on Notion's free plan. You do not need Notion AI, Notion Plus, or any paid Notion subscription to use any part of FreelanceOS. The template also supports team collaboration using Notion's free guest invitations — Notion's free plan includes up to 10 guests, which is sufficient for most solo freelancers building an early-stage team. If you later upgrade to a paid Notion plan, FreelanceOS will benefit from the increased block limits and additional guest slots.
Is FreelanceOS good for freelancers who are starting to build a small team?
▾
Yes, and this is one of FreelanceOS Pro's strongest differentiators. The Team Collaboration workspace lets you invite contractors, virtual assistants, or part-time editors as Notion guests. Each team member can have a filtered view of the Task Board showing only their assigned work — so they don't see your client billing details, proposal drafts, or revenue numbers. You maintain full visibility over all projects and deliverables while giving each team member just enough access to do their job. The system works well for teams of 1 to 5 people, which covers the vast majority of scaling freelance businesses.
