Frequently Asked Questions

Questions From Photographers

Can I track deposits and final payments separately?

Yes. The invoice system in FreelanceOS Pro lets you log a deposit payment and a final balance separately per booking. Each is linked to the client and the shoot record, so you can see at a glance which bookings are deposit-only vs fully paid.

Does it include a client questionnaire system?

Yes. The Client Questionnaire database lets you store pre-shoot questionnaire responses per client — locations, style preferences, outfit choices, timeline notes — all linked to the corresponding shoot record.

How do I track which shoots are still in editing?

The Editing Queue database shows every shoot in post-production with status (culling, editing, color grading, final export), due date, and gallery delivery deadline. Filter by status to see your editing backlog at a glance.

Can I use this for both wedding and portrait photography?

Absolutely. The template is niche-agnostic — it works equally well for wedding, portrait, commercial, real estate, or event photography. You can add custom tags and views per photography type.
