A brand kit is a centralized reference document that captures your visual and verbal identity — your brand colors (with exact hex codes), typography choices, logo variations, tone of voice, and usage guidelines. A Notion brand kit template makes maintaining that consistency effortless because Notion lives in your browser, updates instantly, and can be shared with anyone who needs access.

(3) Logo variations — full color, monochrome, icon-only — with direct file links. (4) Brand voice guidelines covering tone, personality, vocabulary, and what to avoid. (5) Social media template specs with per-platform dimensions and design rules. (6) A brand guidelines document that consolidates everything into a shareable page for collaborators and clients.
