Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a paid Notion plan?

No. FreelanceOS Pro works entirely on Notion's free plan. You don't need Notion Plus or Business to use any of the templates.

How long does setup take?

About 20 minutes. The included Quick Start guide walks you through duplicating the template and entering your first few clients step-by-step.

Can I customize it?

Yes, fully. Every property, view, formula, and page can be renamed, rearranged, or deleted. It's Notion — it's yours to modify.

What if I have more than one type of client?

The CRM uses a Tags property and a Type/Category field so one database handles every kind of client relationship you manage.

Is there a free version?

Yes — FreelanceOS Lite includes a basic client tracker at no cost. Pro adds the full database, all 5 views, and the linked projects, invoices, and time log.
