Upon full payment, all intellectual property rights, including copyrights, trademarks, and patents, in the deliverables shall be transferred to the Client.

Confidentiality

Both parties agree to keep all proprietary information, business data, and project details strictly confidential during and after the contract term.

Non-Compete

The Contractor agrees not to engage in or provide services to direct competitors of the Client for a specified period after contract termination.

Limitation of Liability

The total liability of either party shall not exceed the total fees paid or payable under this agreement. Neither party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages.

Termination Clause

Either party may terminate this agreement with 14 days written notice. Upon termination, the Client shall pay for all work completed up to the termination date.

Force Majeure

Neither party shall be liable for delays or failure to perform due to circumstances beyond their reasonable control, including natural disasters, pandemics, acts of government, or other unforeseeable events.

Indemnification

Each party agrees to indemnify and hold harmless the other party from any claims, damages, losses, or expenses arising from the indemnifying party's breach of this agreement or negligence.

Dispute Resolution

Any disputes arising from this agreement shall be resolved through the selected dispute resolution method before either party may pursue legal action.

Revision Policy

The project includes a specified number of revision rounds. Additional revisions beyond the included rounds will be billed at an agreed-upon rate.

Independent Contractor Status

The Contractor is an independent contractor and not an employee, partner, or agent of the Client. The Contractor is responsible for their own taxes, insurance, and benefits.
