The CPE credit log is purpose-built for certifications that require Continuing Professional Education as a condition of renewal, including CISSP (which requires 120 CPE credits over a three-year cycle plus an annual maintenance fee), CISM (120 CPEs over three years), CISA (120 CPEs over three years), CRISC (120 CPEs over three years), SSCP (60 CPEs over three years), CompTIA CE program certifications like Security+, CySA+, and CASP+, and others.

Each CPE activity entry includes the activity name and description, date completed, provider or organizing body, credit hours earned, CPE category (such as domain-specific security education, professional contribution, self-study, or vendor-sponsored education), and which certification the credits are being claimed toward. For certifications like CISSP that have multiple CPE domains, you can note the primary domain alignment of each activity.

A running CPE total automatically reflects your logged activities so you can see at any moment how many credits you have accumulated and how many remain until your next renewal date. This eliminates the extremely common problem of discovering at renewal time that you are short on credits because you didn't track activities consistently throughout the cycle. The log also helps you plan ahead: if you have 60 of 120 CPEs with 18 months to go, you can see exactly what pace of activity you need to maintain.
