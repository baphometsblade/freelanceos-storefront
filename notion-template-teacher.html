Frequently Asked Questions

Questions From Teachers

How does the lesson plan database work in Notion?

The lesson plan database stores each lesson as a page — with subject, grade level, learning objectives, materials, activities, and notes. You can filter by subject, unit, or date and reuse successful lessons across classes or academic years without starting from scratch.

Can I track individual student progress in this Notion template?

Yes. The student tracker is a database with each student having their own record — assignments submitted, grades, notes from observations, and parent contact history. You get a real picture of where each student stands without shuffling through separate gradebooks.

Is this template suitable for K-12 teachers or higher education?

FreelanceOS Pro adapts to both. K-12 teachers typically use the lesson planner, student tracker, and parent communication log most heavily. Higher education instructors find the curriculum planner, assignment log, and grade book most valuable. The template is flexible enough for either context.

How does the parent communication log work?

The parent communication log is a database of every parent interaction — meeting, email, or phone call — linked to the relevant student. Before parent-teacher conferences, you pull up the log and have a complete record of every conversation, concern, and commitment you've made.
