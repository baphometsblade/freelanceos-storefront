Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a legal case management system or evidence storage system?
No. FreelanceOS Pro is strictly a business operations tool. It tracks your case pipeline, billing, scheduling, and business analytics. It is NOT for storing surveillance footage, subject PII, evidence materials, chain of custody records, or any content subject to evidentiary standards. All investigative files must be maintained in secure, appropriate systems that meet your state PI license requirements and applicable law.
What case types does this template support?
FreelanceOS Pro includes billing and workflow categories for surveillance, background research, asset search, skip trace, insurance fraud, child custody, missing person, corporate due diligence, and process serving coordination. You can customize or add any case types that match your specific practice areas.
Can I track retainers and hourly billing in this template?
Yes. The Client & Case Billing section covers retainer collection, separate hourly rates for field surveillance vs. research, expense reimbursement (mileage at IRS rate, database fees, travel), invoice amounts, and payment tracking — reflecting the real billing complexity of PI work.
Does this work for a solo PI or a multi-investigator agency?
Both. The Surveillance & Field Work Log includes an operative assigned field for team operations. The Sub-Investigator & Contractor Network section helps agency owners track their team. Solo PIs simply leave team fields blank and focus on the billing and business sections most relevant to them.
Does this template require a paid Notion plan?
No. FreelanceOS Pro works with the free Notion plan. You duplicate the template to your own Notion workspace and it's yours to customize. No paid Notion subscription is required to get full value from the template.
