Can I manage multiple client files in one workspace?

Yes. The Client Portal database gives each client their own record with linked files, contact details, engagement type, billing status, and all related deadlines. Filter and search across your entire client list in seconds.

How does the deadline tracker handle recurring tax deadlines?

The Deadline Tracker supports recurring entries so quarterly filings, annual returns, and payroll deadlines automatically reappear each cycle. Color-coded urgency flags highlight what's due within 7, 14, and 30 days.

Does it include a document request system?

Yes. The Document Request System lets you create a checklist of required documents per client engagement. Mark each item as requested, received, or outstanding so you always know what's still needed before you can file.

Can I use this during tax season to manage high client volumes?

Absolutely. The Tax Calendar provides a master view of all client filing deadlines in one place. Sort by due date, filter by filing type, and mark returns as complete without digging through emails or separate spreadsheets.

Does it track billable hours and invoices?

Yes. The Billing Tracker lets you log hourly or flat-fee work per client, track invoice dates and amounts, and mark payments as received or outstanding. Monthly rollups show total billed, collected, and aged receivables.
