📈 Upvote Strategy
  • 🚫
    NEVER buy upvotes. Product Hunt actively detects purchased upvotes and will ban your product — or your entire account. The risk is not worth it and it doesn't work long-term.
  • ✉️
    Ask friends and colleagues to genuinely check it out. Don't say "please upvote my product." Say "I launched FreelanceOS on Product Hunt today — would love for you to check it out and upvote if you find it useful."
  • ⚖️
    PH weighs upvotes by account quality. An upvote from an active, verified PH user counts more than a brand-new account. Encourage your most active tech-community contacts to upvote first.
  • 🤝
    Upvote other products on launch day. Comment genuinely on 5–10 other launches. PH surfaces engaged makers — being active in the community helps your own product get noticed.
  • 🏷️
    Use the Maker badge. Every comment you leave on your own listing shows a "Maker" badge. Respond to everyone — it signals genuine engagement and drives the algorithm.
  • ⏰
    Timing is everything. Launch at 12:01 AM PST (not noon, not afternoon). The full 24 hours of visibility is the single biggest structural advantage you control.
