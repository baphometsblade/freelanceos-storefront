Yes. FreelanceOS Pro is format-agnostic. The Class Planner and Playlist Library work for any group fitness format — yoga, spin, HIIT, Pilates, bootcamp, barre, dance cardio, and more. You customize the fields and programs to match your formats.
Can I track both group classes and private clients in the same system?
Yes. FreelanceOS Pro includes both a Class Planner for group formats and a Client Tracker for private training clients. Both are included in the $29 one-time purchase, and you can use whichever components match your business model.
How do I track CEU credits for multiple certifications?
The Certification Tracker includes a CEU Credit Log where you record each credit earned — provider, date, credits, and type. It links to each certification so you can always see your current progress toward renewal requirements at a glance.
Can I use this to pitch corporate wellness contracts?
The Business Development Hub includes a Corporate Fitness CRM and inquiry pipeline for tracking prospective corporate clients — contact details, follow-up dates, and proposal status. It's designed for exactly this use case and works equally well for pitching employer wellness programs and studio partnerships.
Do I need a paid Notion plan to use all the features?
No. FreelanceOS Pro works with the free Notion plan. Everything is page-based — databases, linked views, and templates — that function at the free tier. You only need a paid Notion plan if you want to add team members or use advanced collaboration features.
