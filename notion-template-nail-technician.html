Frequently Asked Questions

Can I track gel color names so I can match clients on return visits?
Yes. The Appointment Log includes a products used field where you record gel brand and exact gel polish color name per service. When a client returns and wants the same color — or wants something similar — you pull up their last appointment and have the exact reference. No more guessing from memory or apologizing that you can't remember what you used.
Does this work for booth renters, suite renters, and mobile nail techs?
Yes. FreelanceOS Pro is designed for self-employed nail technicians regardless of workspace setup — booth rent, suite rent, or mobile. The Business Dashboard includes a booth/suite rent due date field so your business expenses are visible alongside your revenue. The income tracking, rebooking rate, and client management work the same regardless of where you do your nails.
Can I use this to plan my nail art social media content?
Yes. The Social Media & Content Planning section covers seasonal nail art themes (Valentine's Day, Easter, Halloween, Christmas, summer chrome), trending techniques worth posting (cloud nails, glazed donut, quiet luxury neutrals, 3D flowers), reel and TikTok ideas, hashtag strategy by platform, and a content schedule. Nail tech social media drives bookings when planned intentionally — this system helps you stay ahead of trends instead of reacting to them.
Can I track my nail art designs as a portfolio in this template?
Yes. The Nail Art Design Portfolio & Inspiration Tracker logs each design by name, category, tools used, complexity level (simple/medium/advanced for pricing), products used, and an image reference link from Google Photos or Dropbox. You can assign upcharge amounts per complexity level to turn your design portfolio into a menu with consistent, defensible pricing.
