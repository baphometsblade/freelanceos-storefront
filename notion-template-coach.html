Frequently Asked Questions

Questions From Coaches

Can I share parts of the workspace with my coaching clients?

Yes. Notion's sharing feature lets you share specific filtered views with clients. You can create a client-facing onboarding portal view that shows only their resources, session schedule, and program materials — without exposing your full workspace or other client data.

Does the template work for group coaching programs?

Yes. The Group Coaching Tracker lets you manage cohorts, track individual participant progress within a group, log group session notes, and manage a shared curriculum — separate from your 1:1 client database.

How does the progress tracker work for coaching clients?

The Progress Tracker links to each client record. You log goals set at the start of an engagement, track milestones achieved, note session-by-session wins, and measure movement toward outcomes. It's the evidence base you need when clients ask what they've achieved.

Can I store my coaching frameworks and resources in the template?

Absolutely. The Resource Library is a dedicated database for your frameworks, worksheets, templates, and tools — tagged by topic and linked to client records so you can quickly pull the right resource in any session.
