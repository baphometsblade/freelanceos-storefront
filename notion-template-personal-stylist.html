What's Inside

Six Modules Built for Personal Stylists & Image Consultants

👥

Client Style Profile Database

Per client: measurements and fit preferences, color palette (colors they love and colors to avoid), style aesthetic (classic, minimalist, maximalist, bohemian, streetwear, business), lifestyle context (work environment, social calendar, travel frequency), budget range, brands they love, brands to avoid, body confidence notes, and Pinterest or reference board link. The complete brief for every session.

📸

Wardrobe Audit Module

Catalog key items per client: item category (tops, bottoms, outerwear, shoes, bags, accessories), color, brand, size, condition (keep/donate/repair/replace), and outfit pairing potential. After an audit session you have a complete searchable record of each client's existing wardrobe — essential for targeted personal shopping and capsule wardrobe recommendations.

🛒

Shopping List Manager

Per-client item lists with: item description, category, budget per item, priority (need vs want vs wishlist), stores or websites to check, found (yes/no), purchased (yes/no), and price paid. When personal shopping for multiple clients in a single week, the filtered view keeps each client's list separate, prioritized, and tracked through to purchase.

📖

Lookbook & Outfit Planner

Per client: outfit combinations built from their wardrobe (item list or image reference links), occasion (daily work, casual weekend, travel, events, date night), season, outfit status (proposed/approved/styled), client feedback, and photo links. Build digital lookbooks your clients can reference between sessions — the deliverable that justifies retainer relationships.

🔗

Brand Affiliate & Partnership Tracker

Per affiliate relationship: brand, affiliate platform (LTK, ShopStyle, Amazon, brand direct), commission rate, payment threshold, payment schedule, and income to date. Track this growing revenue stream alongside your service income to understand the total value of your styling business and which brands are worth nurturing.

💼

Styling Business Dashboard

Active clients, monthly revenue, income by service type (wardrobe audit, personal shopping, capsule build, outfit planning, event styling, image consultation, retainer), average package value, new client inquiry conversion rate, referral source breakdown (Instagram, word of mouth, corporate referral, website), and annual revenue goal — your business at a glance.
