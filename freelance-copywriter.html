FAQ

Questions from copywriters specifically

Can I manage retainer clients and one-off projects in the same system?

Yes — that's one of the key things FreelanceOS Pro was designed for. The Client Tracker lets you flag each client as retainer or project-based. The Finance Dashboard splits your income accordingly so you can see your stable MRR vs. variable project income at any time. You can also set recurring invoice reminders for retainer clients so billing is never an afterthought.

Can I store client brand voice guides and brief notes inside the system?

Yes. Each client in the CRM has a dedicated notes section where you can paste brand guidelines, tone rules, target audience summaries, forbidden words, and any brief context. Since it's Notion, you can embed tables, bullet lists, or even link to external Figma or Google Docs files from the same client record.

Does this work for email copywriters, SEO writers, and brand copywriters alike?

Yes. The system is format-agnostic — deliverable names are fully customizable, so you can call them "email sequence," "blog post," "landing page copy," or whatever fits your work. The approval status, invoicing, and client management layers work identically regardless of the content type.
