Recommended tools

8 tools that work well alongside Notion

These are not our products. They're genuinely useful apps we recommend to freelancers and creators who use Notion — tools that fill in the gaps Notion doesn't cover natively.

✏️
NotionFree plan
Workspace · Foundation
The workspace that powers everything on this page. Notion's free plan covers unlimited blocks, 10 guests per workspace, and the full database feature set — enough for most freelancers indefinitely.
Visit Notion
⚡
ZapierFree plan
Automation · Workflows
Automate repetitive tasks between Notion and the rest of your stack — create Notion tasks from new emails, log new clients from form submissions, or sync invoices to your accounting app automatically.
Visit Zapier
🔄
Make (formerly Integromat)Free plan
Automation · Advanced
More powerful than Zapier for complex multi-step automations, with a generous free tier. Great for connecting Notion to payment processors, form tools, and project management apps with custom logic.
Visit Make
⏲
Toggl TrackFree plan
Time tracking
The simplest time tracker for freelancers. Start a timer, tag it to a client and project, and export reports for billing. Free plan covers unlimited projects and clients — pairs naturally with FreelanceOS.
Visit Toggl Track
💲
Harvest
Time tracking · Invoicing
If you want time tracking and invoicing in one tool (rather than managing invoices in Notion), Harvest is the cleanest option for freelancers. Connects well with Notion via Zapier for status syncing.
Visit Harvest
📈
FreshBooks
Accounting · Invoicing
Accounting software purpose-built for freelancers and small businesses. Handles the parts that Notion can't — tax prep, expense tracking, actual invoice sending with payment portals. Use alongside FreelanceOS.
Visit FreshBooks
🎥
LoomFree plan
Video · Client communication
Record quick screen-share walkthroughs for clients instead of writing long status emails. Store Loom links directly in your FreelanceOS project cards for async client updates that actually get watched.
Visit Loom
📅
CalendlyFree plan
Scheduling · Booking
Let clients book discovery calls and project check-ins without the back-and-forth. Add your Calendly link to your client portal page in Notion so clients can self-schedule without emailing you.
Visit Calendly
Disclosure: None of these are affiliate links. We don't earn anything from recommending these tools — we just think they're genuinely useful for freelancers and creators using Notion.
