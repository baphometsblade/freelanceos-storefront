FreelanceOS vs HoneyBook

The polished all-in-one for creative businesses

HoneyBook is a well-funded SaaS platform designed for creative entrepreneurs and service providers. It offers a slick interface with built-in payment processing, scheduling, and automations. It is one of the most popular freelance tools on the market.

HoneyBook Pricing

Starter $19/mo · Essentials $39/mo · Premium $79/mo
Annual billing saves ~17%

Best For

Photographers, event planners, and creatives who need built-in payment processing and client booking in one tool.

HoneyBook Pros

  • Built-in payment processing (ACH, cards)
  • Beautiful client-facing proposals and contracts
  • Scheduling and automations baked in
  • Polished native mobile app
  • 1,000+ integrations via Zapier

HoneyBook Cons

  • $228–$948/year recurring cost
  • No time tracking
  • No tax tools or estimated tax calculator
  • Limited customization compared to Notion
  • Locked into their ecosystem
The Verdict: Choose HoneyBook if you need built-in payment processing and automated client booking. Choose FreelanceOS if you want a one-time payment, full customization, and 91 free tools. Over 12 months, HoneyBook costs 13–54x more than FreelanceOS Pro.

Freelancers with high client volume who need automated onboarding workflows and form-based lead capture.

  • Strong scheduler with calendar sync

Dubsado Cons

  • $240–$480/year recurring cost
  • Steep learning curve
  • No time tracking
  • No tax tools
  • Interface can feel dated compared to newer tools
The Verdict: Choose Dubsado if you need complex workflow automations and automated client onboarding. Choose FreelanceOS if you prefer simplicity, one-time pricing, and the flexibility of Notion. FreelanceOS pays for itself in the first month vs Dubsado's recurring fees.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is FreelanceOS really a one-time payment?
Yes. FreelanceOS Pro is a single payment of $17.40 (with the current flash sale) or $29 at full price. There are no monthly fees, no annual renewals, and no hidden charges. Once you buy it, the Notion template is yours forever. Compare that to HoneyBook at $19–79/month or Dubsado at $20–40/month.
Can FreelanceOS replace HoneyBook or Dubsado?
For most freelancers, yes. FreelanceOS covers client management, project tracking, invoicing, contracts, proposals, and financial dashboards. The main things it cannot replace are built-in payment processing (you would still need Stripe, PayPal, or similar) and automated client booking with calendar integration. If those features are essential to your workflow, HoneyBook or Dubsado are better fits.
What are the 91 free tools included with FreelanceOS?
FreelanceOS offers 91 browser-based tools at no cost and no signup required. These include an invoice generator, contract builder, proposal writer, tax calculator, rate calculator, scope-of-work generator, and dozens more. Visit the 91 Free Tools page to explore them all.
Does FreelanceOS work on mobile?
FreelanceOS runs inside Notion, which has mobile apps for iOS and Android. So yes, you can access your FreelanceOS workspace on mobile through the Notion app. However, unlike HoneyBook or Dubsado, FreelanceOS does not have its own standalone mobile app.
How does FreelanceOS pricing compare to HoneyBook?
HoneyBook starts at $19/month ($228/year) for their Starter plan. FreelanceOS Pro costs $17.40 one-time with the flash sale, or $29 at full price. That means FreelanceOS pays for itself in less than one month compared to HoneyBook, and you never pay again.
Is FreelanceOS good for agencies or teams?
FreelanceOS is designed for solo freelancers and small teams (2–5 people). Since it runs on Notion, you can share your workspace with collaborators if you have a Notion team plan. However, if you need multi-user role permissions, automated team workflows, or enterprise features, tools like Dubsado or HoneyBook may be a better fit.
What if I don't use Notion?
FreelanceOS requires Notion (the free plan works fine). If you prefer not to use Notion, you would be better served by a standalone SaaS tool like HoneyBook, Bonsai, or Moxie. However, Notion's free plan is generous, and many freelancers already use it for notes and project management. You might find you enjoy the flexibility.
Can I try FreelanceOS before buying?
Absolutely. You have three ways to try before buying: (1) FreelanceOS Lite is completely free and includes project tracking, a basic client list, invoice overview, and weekly planner. (2) You have access to 91 free browser-based tools with no signup required. (3) FreelanceOS Pro comes with a 30-day money-back guarantee, so you can try it risk-free.
