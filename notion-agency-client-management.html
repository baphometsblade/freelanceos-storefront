The Client Onboarding System

AgencyOS Pro includes a standardized client onboarding workflow that every new client moves through. This is the most valuable SOP in an agency — inconsistent onboarding is the number one cause of early client churn.

  1. Contract signed: Client record created in Notion. Team notified. Kickoff meeting scheduled.
  2. Kickoff meeting: Structured agenda template with goals, success metrics, communication preferences, and stakeholder introductions. Notes auto-link to client record.
  3. Client setup: Shared workspace permissions set. Communication channel created. Reporting cadence confirmed. Access credentials collected.
  4. First deliverable: Project created in Notion with scope, timeline, and team assignments from the project template.
  5. Day 30 check-in: NPS pulse survey and progress review. Any early issues surfaced and resolved before they become churn risks.

Team Task Assignment and Workload Management

The hardest operational problem for boutique agencies is knowing whether team members have too much or too little work. AgencyOS Pro's Team Capacity dashboard shows:

  • Every active task assigned to each team member with effort estimate
  • Total committed hours for the week vs. available hours
  • Red/yellow/green capacity indicator per person
  • Tasks without an assignee (orphaned work) flagged automatically
  • Upcoming deadlines that require capacity allocation in the next two weeks

This eliminates the three most common agency operational failures: accidentally over-promising on delivery because you didn't know the team was at capacity, underutilizing team members who have bandwidth, and missing deadlines because work got stuck in an owner's backlog with no visibility.
