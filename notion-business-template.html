The 6 Databases You Need

A real Notion business OS is built on six interconnected databases. Here is what each one does and why the connections between them matter:

  • Clients DB: Every contact, company, deal value, and pipeline stage. The hub everything else connects to.
  • Projects DB: Every active and past project, linked to the client who hired you. Tracks status, deadlines, and deliverables.
  • Tasks DB: Every to-do item linked to a project. Powers your daily task view filtered by what is due today.
  • Finance DB: Invoices and expenses, linked to clients and projects. Formula-calculated totals and a monthly revenue rollup.
  • Content DB: Every piece of content planned, in progress, or published. Multi-platform with status tracking and idea linking.
  • SOPs DB: Every repeatable process documented as a step-by-step page. Referenced from Projects and linked to the tasks they generate.
