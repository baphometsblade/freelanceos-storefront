Can I use this template without any prior Notion experience?

Yes. The included setup guide assumes zero prior Notion knowledge. It walks you through duplicating the template, understanding the database structure, and adding your first client and project. If you can use a spreadsheet, you have enough technical skill to use FreelanceOS Pro. The template's databases are pre-configured — you do not need to build views, create formulas, or set up relations yourself. Everything is ready; you just add your data.

FreelanceOS Pro is optimized for service-based work (client relationships, project deliverables, invoicing). Most solopreneurs with mixed service/product revenue use FreelanceOS for the service side and a separate Stripe or Gumroad dashboard for product revenue. The two work well in parallel.
