What's Inside

8 Modules That Organize Your Entire Practice

Every component is built for the specific workflow of a financial advisor — not repurposed from a generic productivity template. Each module connects to the others so your practice data flows naturally.

Module 1

CE Credit Tracker

Never scramble before a renewal deadline again. The CE tracker is the centerpiece of the template — designed specifically for the multi-license reality of most financial advisors who simultaneously manage CFP Board CE, FINRA continuing education, state insurance CE, and designation-specific requirements.

  • Hours earned per category tracked against your requirement (ethics, planning, investment, insurance, tax)
  • Required hours per license type with color-coded progress toward each cycle deadline
  • Credit provider log: course name, provider, date completed, credit hours, license it applies to
  • Renewal date countdown with automatic "at risk" flag when you are within 60 days and under pace
  • Multi-license support: run CFP, Series 65/66, CFA, ChFC, CLU, and state insurance simultaneously
  • Carryover credit notes for CE that applies across multiple requirements in the same period
  • Annual summary view to document your completed CE for any compliance audit or firm requirement

Module 2

License & Designation Hub

Every license, every designation, every renewal date in one place. From your FINRA-registered licenses to your professional certifications, the hub keeps you organized across the full span of your credentials — and fires reminders before deadlines sneak up on you.

  • Series 7, 63, 65, 66, and other FINRA registrations with renewal and U4 update reminders
  • CFP, CFA, ChFC, CLU, RICP, and other designation renewal tracking
  • State securities license expiration dates with renewal fee notes
  • State insurance license tracking across multiple states (life, health, variable annuity lines)
  • E&O insurance policy renewal dates and coverage limit records (your own coverage, not client data)
  • Designation exam progress tracker for credentials currently in progress
  • Historical log of all credentials earned, exam dates, and study hours invested

Module 3

Business Development Pipeline

Your business development needs a visual pipeline just like any sales process — but built for how financial advisors actually grow their practices: through referrals, COI relationships, and deliberate follow-up over months or years. This module gives you that structure. Note: this tracks prospects by name/relationship stage only — no financial information is stored here.

  • Prospect stages: Introduced → Initial Contact → Discovery Scheduled → Proposal → Decision → Closed/Inactive
  • Referral source tracking: which COI, which channel, which relationship generated the introduction
  • COI relationship management: attorney, CPA, estate planning, real estate contacts and last-touch log
  • Follow-up calendar with priority flags for prospects at critical decision stages
  • Prospect stage velocity: see how long people spend in each stage and where your pipeline stalls
  • Annual business development goal tracking: new household target, referral target, COI meeting target
  • Pipeline value estimate column for annual planning (your estimate only — no client financial data)

  • COI contact database: CPAs, estate attorneys, P&C agents, real estate agents, divorce attorneys
  • Referral partner tier system: A-tier (active referrers), B-tier (warm), C-tier (developing)

  • COI meeting planner: quarterly touchpoint calendar to stay systematically in front of your top partners

How It Works

Set Up in 30 Minutes, Run for Years

The template is designed to start delivering value immediately — you do not need to learn Notion deeply before you benefit.

1

Set Up Your License Cycle

Duplicate the template into your Notion workspace. Enter each of your active licenses and designations — Series licenses, CFP, CFA, state insurance — with their renewal dates and CE requirements for the current cycle. The CE tracker immediately shows you where you stand against each requirement. This step takes 15–20 minutes and you will never have to rebuild it from scratch again.

2

Log CE Credits as You Earn Them

After each course, webinar, conference session, or self-study activity, spend 90 seconds logging it in the CE tracker: course name, provider, date, hours earned, credit category, and which license it applies to. The tracker updates your running total automatically. By the time your renewal arrives, your documentation is already complete — no frantic scrambling, no hunting through email confirmations.

3

Track Business Development Alongside Compliance

Open your practice hub each Monday to see the week's compliance deadlines alongside your business development follow-ups and COI touchpoints. The unified view means you never separate the operational side of your practice from the growth side. Check your pipeline stages, log new professional contacts from the week, update your marketing content calendar, and start the week with complete visibility over your entire practice in one window.

Frequently Asked Questions

Questions From Financial Advisors

Real questions from advisors who asked before purchasing — especially about compliance and appropriate use.

❓Can I store client financial data in this Notion template?
Absolutely not, and this cannot be emphasized strongly enough. This template is designed exclusively for your own professional organization — CE credits, license renewals, your business development pipeline, and practice administration. It must never be used to store client account numbers, portfolio holdings, account values, Social Security numbers, birth dates, or any other client financial data. All client financial data must remain in your firm's SEC/FINRA-compliant CRM or portfolio management software. Notion is not a compliant platform for regulated financial data. If you are unsure whether something belongs in this template, assume it does not and consult your compliance department.
❓Does this Notion template replace my CRM?
No — and it is not designed to. This template contains a Professional Network CRM module for tracking COIs (centers of influence), referral partners, study group members, and conference connections — non-client professional contacts only. Your actual client relationships, portfolio data, account information, and client interactions must remain in your firm's compliant CRM (Salesforce Financial Services Cloud, Redtail, Wealthbox, or similar). Think of this template as the layer that organizes your own professional life, not your clients' financial lives.
❓Does this work for independent RIAs and wirehouse advisors?
Yes — the template is designed to be useful whether you are an independent RIA, a fee-only advisor, a wirehouse rep (Merrill, Morgan Stanley, Wells Fargo, UBS), a broker-dealer registered representative, or a dual-registrant. The CE credit tracker handles requirements from FINRA (Series 7, 63, 65, 66) and CFP Board, CFA Institute, The American College (ChFC, CLU, RICP), and state insurance CE authorities. The business development pipeline and professional network modules apply regardless of your firm affiliation. Independent advisors may find it especially useful since they do not have firm infrastructure to lean on for practice management organization.
❓Is this template useful for a financial advisor just starting out?
Absolutely. In fact, building your organization system from day one is one of the best habits a new advisor can develop. The CE tracker helps you stay on top of requirements while you are in your training years and potentially sitting for multiple designations simultaneously. The business development pipeline helps you build a referral network intentionally from the start. The license hub ensures no renewal deadline ever catches you off guard during those early years when you may have multiple licenses and designations in various stages. Starting organized is exponentially easier than trying to retrofit a system onto years of accumulated chaos.
❓Can a team of advisors share this Notion template?
Yes. With a Notion Plus or Business workspace, multiple team members can share the same workspace. Each advisor can have their own CE tracker, license hub, and professional development log as private pages, while sharing a common marketing content planner, team admin calendar, or firm vendor management database. Team leads and ensemble practice principals can use the template structure to track CE compliance across their team. Just ensure that no shared views are used to track client financial data — all advisors remain individually responsible for keeping regulated data in compliant systems.
❓Does this template track CE credits for multiple licenses simultaneously?
Yes, and this is one of the features advisors tell us they value most. The CE Credit Tracker is built to handle multiple license types at once — FINRA-regulated licenses (Series 7, 63, 65, 66) with their Regulatory Element requirements, CFP Board continuing education (30 hours per two-year cycle, including 2 hours mandatory ethics), CFA Institute local society requirements, ChFC and CLU designations through The American College (30 hours per two-year period), and state insurance license CE which varies by state. Each license or designation gets its own requirement set, renewal date, and credit category breakdown so you can track progress toward each independently without losing track of the others.
