How to Set It Up

Set Up Your Weekly Review System in 30 Minutes

The setup is front-loaded. Do it once, use it every week for years.

1

Duplicate the template into your Notion workspace

Click the duplicate link and choose your workspace. The full system — all databases, linked views, and formulas — copies in about 30 seconds.

2

Add your existing clients and active projects

Spend 10 minutes populating your client database with current clients and your project database with active work. This unlocks the linked views in the weekly review.

3

Set a recurring Friday reminder

Block 20 minutes every Friday afternoon. The review template creates a new entry for each week with a single button click — everything resets automatically.

4

Link your invoices so revenue pulls automatically

Connect the invoice database to your client records. The weekly review's revenue section will show your week's billing status without any manual entry.

5

Run your first review and adjust the template to your workflow

The template is a starting point. Add or remove sections based on what matters to your specific practice. Most users find their rhythm by week 3.

FAQ

Common Questions

How long does a weekly review take with this template?

Most users settle into 15–25 minutes. The first few reviews take longer as you figure out what matters most to you. By week four, it becomes a fast, almost meditative routine — you're not deciding what to review, just filling in what happened.

Does the free version include the weekly review system?

The free tier includes a basic weekly planner. The Pro version adds the full weekly review with revenue pulls from your invoice database, the quarterly tracker, the energy/focus rating, and the linked client status overview. If you do more than 2–3 active projects at a time, Pro's connected views save significant time.

Can I run a monthly review in the same system?

Yes. FreelanceOS Pro includes a monthly review template as a separate database that rolls up data from your weekly reviews. It shows month-over-month revenue trends, top clients by billing, and the recurring blockers that showed up most often in your weekly reviews.

What if I already have a Notion workspace I'm happy with?

You can add just the weekly review component to your existing workspace. The template is modular — you don't have to adopt the whole FreelanceOS system. That said, the weekly review gets significantly more powerful when it's connected to the client, project, and invoice databases.

Is there a mobile version?

Notion's mobile app works well for doing quick weekly reviews on your phone. The linked views and databases all load on mobile. For detailed planning work, most users prefer desktop, but a quick Friday review from your phone is entirely workable.
