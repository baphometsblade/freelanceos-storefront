Step 1: First Contact Signals
How did this client first reach out to you?
📬 How did they find you?
💰 Did they mention budget upfront?
⏰ How quickly do they want to start?
📋 Do they have a clear brief or project scope?
🤝 Have they worked with freelancers before?
💬 How do they communicate?
Step 2: Negotiation Red Flags
Warning signs during the deal-making phase.
🎨 Did they ask for free spec work or a "test project"?
📢 "It'll be great exposure / for your portfolio"?
🔄 Do they want unlimited revisions?
📉 Are they pushing for below-market rates?
🌫️ Is the scope vague or constantly shifting?
🎰 Want to pay "when the project makes money"?
⚖️ Comparing you to cheaper freelancers?
🔬 Already micromanaging before the project starts?
Step 3: Gut Check
Trust your instincts. How does this client make you feel?
🗓️ Do they respect your timeline and availability?
🗣️ Have they badmouthed previous freelancers?
🧭 Do they understand and respect your process?
📱 Are they responsive to your messages?
💎 Do they value your expertise or treat you like a tool?
