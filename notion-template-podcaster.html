Frequently Asked Questions

Questions From Podcasters

How does the episode planner work in Notion?

The episode planner is a database with each episode as a record — topic, guest, recording date, publish date, status, and show notes linked. You can see your full publishing pipeline in a kanban or calendar view and plan multiple episodes ahead without losing track.

Can I manage podcast guest outreach in CreatorHQ?

Yes. The guest CRM tracks every potential guest with their contact info, outreach status, response, confirmed date, and episode linked. No more hunting through email to figure out who you've pitched and who's waiting on a follow-up.

Does CreatorHQ include a sponsor tracking system?

Yes. The sponsor and ad tracker logs every sponsor relationship — company, campaign dates, deliverables, ad reads due, and payment status. You'll always know what's owed to sponsors and what revenue is coming in from each relationship.

How do I use the content repurposing board?

The content repurposing board is a kanban where each episode card tracks the repurposed content derived from it — clip for social, quote graphic, blog post, email newsletter. Each piece has a status and channel so nothing falls through after the episode goes live.
