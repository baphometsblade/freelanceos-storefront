Is this Notion template designed specifically for authors and writers?
Yes — every database and workflow is built around the author's work cycle: project ideation, manuscript drafting, scene planning, character building, querying, publishing, and marketing. It works for fiction, nonfiction, and self-published authors alike.
Do I need a paid Notion plan to use this?
No. FreelanceOS Pro is built on Notion's free plan features only. No Notion AI is required. Just duplicate the template into your free Notion workspace and start using it immediately.
Can I track multiple books and writing projects at once?
Yes — the Manuscript Tracker supports unlimited projects. Each project has its own word count goals, chapter breakdown, scene planner, and linked character database. Filter by genre, status, or deadline to stay organized across a series or multiple standalone works.
Does it help with tracking literary agent queries?
Yes. The Query Tracker lets you log each agent queried with their agency, submission date, materials sent, response received, and current status. You can track simultaneous submissions across dozens of agents and follow up on time.
Can self-published authors use this too?
Absolutely. The Publishing Pipeline includes a self-publishing workflow covering cover design, formatting, ISBN procurement, upload to platforms (KDP, IngramSpark, Draft2Digital), pricing strategy, and launch planning. The Royalties Log tracks income by platform.
