The Planning Process

From annual vision to today's priorities in three steps

FreelanceOS Pro uses a cascading structure so your biggest goals always connect to your most immediate actions. No goal ever gets orphaned in a wishlist again.

1

Set Your Annual Vision

Open the Annual Goals module and define the 3 to 5 outcomes that make this year feel like a success. You'll set a revenue milestone alongside a personal milestone — because your income target and your life target should sit in the same plan. Add a "why" for each goal so when momentum dips, the purpose stays visible.

Takes ~45 minutes
2

Break Down the Quarters

Distribute your annual goals across four quarters with specific milestones per quarter. Each quarter gets a theme that guides both your client pipeline and your personal focus. The quarterly structure makes your annual vision feel achievable — and the built-in review prompts keep you honest about what's actually working every 90 days.

Takes ~30 minutes per quarter
3

Execute Weekly

Each Monday, open your Weekly Planning view. Your quarterly milestone surfaces automatically. You assign your top three work priorities, your one non-negotiable life goal, and your energy forecast for the week. Then daily rituals keep you on track day-by-day. The system does the heavy lifting — you show up and execute.

Takes ~15 min/week
