Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a pesticide application record system for license compliance?
No. FreelanceOS Pro is a business operations tool for account scheduling, billing, inventory management, and business analytics. Detailed pesticide application records required by your state pesticide applicator license must be maintained in systems that meet your regulatory requirements. This template manages your service business operations, not your compliance documentation.
Can I track both residential and commercial accounts?
Yes. The Client Account & Service Schedule supports residential, commercial restaurant, commercial food processing, commercial office, industrial, and retail property types. Each account type has relevant fields for service frequency, access notes, and contract terms. You can filter and sort by property type to manage your commercial accounts separately from residential.
Does this help with recurring route planning?
Yes. The Recurring Service Route & Calendar lets you pull accounts due each week, sort them by neighborhood for geographic clustering, note access requirements (key in lockbox, gate codes, call-ahead), and list materials needed per stop. Tight geographic routing is one of the most impactful levers for reducing cost per service call in a pest control business.
Can I track my monthly recurring revenue from service contracts?
Yes. The Business Dashboard tracks accounts on service contract, monthly recurring revenue, new accounts signed this month, account cancellations, and net account growth — the core metrics for a recurring-revenue pest control business. You can also see the split between recurring and one-time revenue to understand your business stability.
Does this template require a paid Notion plan?
No. FreelanceOS Pro works with the free Notion plan. You duplicate the template to your Notion workspace and it is immediately ready to use. No paid Notion subscription is required to access all template features.
