🚀 Getting Started

How do I get my template after purchasing?

After completing your purchase, you'll be redirected to a thank-you page with your Notion access link. You'll also receive a delivery email with the link.

Click the link → in Notion, click "Duplicate" in the top-right corner → the template is added to your workspace. Takes less than 60 seconds.

Do I need to know how to use Notion first?

No prior Notion experience needed. FreelanceOS is designed with beginners in mind — every section has a short intro explaining what it's for and how to use it.

Most people are up and running within 30 minutes. If you've used any spreadsheet or project management tool, you'll feel at home immediately.

Does FreelanceOS work on mobile?

Yes. Notion has full-featured iOS and Android apps. The FreelanceOS template is designed to work on mobile — you can log client calls, update project statuses, and check invoices from your phone.

The more complex views (Finance Dashboard, Kanban) are better on desktop, but the core workflow is fully mobile-friendly.

How long does setup take?

Most freelancers are fully set up in under 2 hours. The typical path:

  • Day 1 (30 min) — Duplicate template, add existing clients, set up your service packages
  • Day 2 (30 min) — Import or recreate active projects, customize status labels
  • Day 3 (30 min) — Set up finance categories, add your first invoice template

After that it becomes daily habit — a quick 5-minute check-in is all you need to keep everything current.
