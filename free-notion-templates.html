Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a paid Notion plan to use these templates?
No. Every free template on this page works with the Notion free plan. Notion's free tier includes unlimited pages, basic databases, and all the core features needed to use any template here. You do not need a Plus, Business, or Enterprise plan.
How do I use a Notion template?
Using a Notion template takes 3 steps: (1) Click the download or "Get Template" link on this page. (2) You'll be taken to a Notion page — click "Duplicate" in the top-right corner of the page. (3) The template is now in your Notion workspace and ready to customize. That's it. No import process, no CSV uploads.
Can I customize the templates?
Yes, fully. All Notion templates are completely customizable — you can rename databases, add or remove properties, change views, adjust colors, and modify any content. Think of the template as a starting structure, not a fixed format. Most users customize their copy within the first few days of using it.
Are the free templates really free?
Yes. FreelanceOS Lite costs $0. The 12 interactive tools on this page cost $0. The standalone templates (client tracker, onboarding checklist, starter pack) cost $0. No hidden fees, no paywall after download, no email required for most tools. We offer a paid upgrade (FreelanceOS Pro at $29) for freelancers who want the full linked system, but the free versions are complete and functional on their own.
What's the difference between the free templates and FreelanceOS Pro?
The free templates are standalone databases and tools. FreelanceOS Pro is a fully integrated system — 8 linked databases (clients, projects, invoices, contacts, tasks, time log, goals, and expenses) that all talk to each other, plus a pre-built business dashboard, 5 product templates, a 30-minute setup video, and lifetime access to all future updates.
