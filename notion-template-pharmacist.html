FAQ

Questions From Pharmacists

How does the CPD and CE hours tracker work?

Each continuing education activity is a record with course name, provider, date, credit hours, topic area, and accreditation type. Rollup formulas sum your completed hours against your renewal target automatically so you always know your current standing without counting manually.

Can this be used to store patient data or medication records?

No. This template is strictly for personal professional development and career organization. It is not HIPAA-compliant and must never be used to store patient data, prescription records, medication histories, or any protected health information. Always use your facility or employer HIPAA-compliant systems for anything patient-related.

Is this useful for locum pharmacists specifically?

Locum pharmacists find the multi-location income tracker, license renewal calendar for multiple state licenses, and consulting client log especially valuable. You can compare assignments, track income per locum engagement, and manage credentials across jurisdictions in one organized place.

How does the consulting client tracker work?

Each consulting client has a record with organization, contact, project scope, engagement dates, deliverables, invoice status, and notes. You can manage multiple consulting relationships simultaneously and know at a glance which clients need follow-up and which projects are in progress.

How does the salary negotiation prep section work?

The Salary Negotiation Prep section lets you log market salary data, your accomplishments and impact metrics, competing offers, and negotiation notes. Having your evidence organized before a salary conversation means you go in prepared, confident, and data-driven rather than guessing at a number.

Can I track a side business like a compounding pharmacy or consulting practice?

Yes. The Side Business Tracker lets you log clients, revenue, expenses, and project status for consulting, compounding, med sync coaching, or any other pharmacy-adjacent venture. Separate from your main employment income, it gives you a clear picture of your total professional earnings.

Is this useful for pharmacy students or residents?

Absolutely. Pharmacy students use the rotation tracker, certification planner, and professional networking sections to stay organized through their APPE rotations. Residents add sections for research project milestones, abstract submissions, and residency project deadlines. The template is fully customizable to your current stage.
