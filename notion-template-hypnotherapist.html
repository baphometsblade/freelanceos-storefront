Client Appointment Overview (Administrative)

Session & Program Log (Administrative Billing)

Administrative billing record only — no session content, no induction records, no therapeutic disclosures stored.

Structured program portfolio: program name (Quit Smoking in 1 Session, Sleep Restoration 5-Session, Confidence Boost 3-Session, etc.), sessions included, price, client's self-stated target outcome, active clients on this program, completion rate, success rate (client-reported outcomes at 30-day follow-up), revenue this quarter. Your program performance at a glance.

Audio & Resource Library

Hypnotherapy audio recordings and resources you've created: recording title, purpose (sleep/anxiety/confidence/habit change — general), format (MP3/Spotify/private YouTube/Bandcamp), whether it's sold or included with programs, sales count, client feedback themes. Your intellectual property catalog with revenue data — know which recordings generate the most passive income.

Marketing & Practice Growth

Lead sources (Google/"hypnotherapy near me"/Psychology Today/Hypnotherapy Society directory/GP referral/Instagram/word of mouth/podcast guest), lead volume by source, consultation call conversion rate, professional associations for credibility (NCH, GHR, BSCH, ABH, NGH, BWRT Institute), CPD log (hours per year required, completed), practitioner supervision log (if required by accreditation body).

Active clients in programs, session volume this month, revenue by service type (individual sessions/programs/audio products/group workshops/online courses), revenue vs. goal, client inquiry to booking conversion rate, session no-show rate, program completion rate, 30-day outcome follow-up rate, professional association standing, CPD status, annual revenue goal.
