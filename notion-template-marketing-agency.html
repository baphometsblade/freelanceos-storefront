Frequently Asked Questions

Questions From Marketing Agencies

Can I manage multiple client campaigns at the same time in Notion?

Yes. AgencyOS includes a campaign planner with filters by client, status, and channel. Every campaign is linked to its client record, deliverables, and team tasks — so you can see across all active work without switching contexts.

How does the client approval workflow work?

The client approval workflow is a Notion page per deliverable with a status field: In Progress → Under Review → Client Approved → Published. You share the review page with clients, they leave comments, and you mark approval — keeping the entire history in one place.

Does AgencyOS support retainer billing tracking?

Yes. The retainer tracker logs monthly retainer amount, renewal date, scope, and actual hours or deliverables delivered per month. It's the early warning system for scope creep before it becomes a client conversation.

How many team members can use AgencyOS?

AgencyOS is a Notion template — anyone on your Notion workspace can access it. There's no per-seat licensing. If you're on the Notion free plan, you're limited to 10 guests; Notion Plus supports unlimited team members.
