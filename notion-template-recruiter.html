A Complete Recruiting OS — Sourcing to Placement in One Workspace

FreelanceOS Pro for recruiters connects your entire workflow — from first contact through placed and paid — in a single, searchable Notion workspace.

Candidate Pipeline Board (Kanban)

A visual pipeline from Sourced to Placed. Each candidate card shows role, client, current stage, and next action. Filter by client, role, or stage — and know at a glance which candidates need attention today and which are moving smoothly.

Client Job Requisitions Tracker

Each open role gets its own record with client name, role title, requirements, salary band, headcount, deadline, and linked candidates. See which roles are fully staffed, which need a push, and which clients need an update call.

Interview Scheduling System

Log every interview round per candidate — date, interviewer, format (phone, video, on-site), and outcome. Never accidentally send a candidate to a round they've already completed or miss a scheduled debrief with a hiring manager.

Offer Negotiation Tracker

Track every offer in flight — candidate, role, offer amount, counter, acceptance status, and start date. Compare offers across roles to identify patterns in what's closing and what's falling apart at the final stage.

Talent Pool Database

A searchable database of passive candidates tagged by skill set, seniority, location, and availability. When a new requisition opens, search your existing talent pool first — your best candidates are already in your network.

Placement Metrics Dashboard

Track time-to-fill, offer acceptance rate, and placement count per client and per role type. Identify your highest-converting clients and the requisitions where you consistently struggle — then optimize accordingly.

Commission Tracker

Every placement linked to its commission — fee percentage, gross amount, invoice date, and payment status. Rollup formulas calculate year-to-date commissions and outstanding balances automatically, so you always know your exact number.

Candidate Feedback Log

Store interviewer feedback per candidate and round. Reference past feedback when making placement decisions, use it to coach candidates before final rounds, and keep a record if a client disputes a placement later.

FAQ

Questions From Recruiters

How does the candidate pipeline board work?

The Candidate Pipeline is a Kanban board with stages from Sourced through Placed. Each candidate card shows role, client, status, and next action. You can filter by client, role, or stage and see your entire active pipeline at a glance without digging through a spreadsheet.

Can I track multiple job requisitions for different clients?

Yes. The Client Job Requisitions Tracker gives each open role its own record — client, role title, requirements, target salary, headcount, deadline, and linked candidates. You see which roles are closest to being filled and where you need to source harder.

Does this replace an ATS like Greenhouse or Lever?

FreelanceOS Pro is a personal business management system, not a full enterprise ATS. Independent recruiters and small agencies use it as an organized alternative to spreadsheets. For clients who mandate a specific ATS, you'd use this alongside it to manage your own workflow and commission tracking.

How does the commission tracker work?

Each placement has a record with the candidate, client, placed salary, fee percentage, gross commission, and payment status. Rollup formulas calculate your year-to-date commissions and outstanding balances automatically — so you always know your number.

Can I manage a passive talent pool for future roles?

Yes. The Talent Pool Database holds candidates who aren't right for a current role but should be contacted when something fits. Tag each person by skill, seniority, and availability so you can filter and find the right match instantly when a new requisition opens.

How does the interview scheduling system work?

Each candidate record has an interview log with round, date, interviewer, format, and outcome. You can see every candidate's interview status at a glance and never accidentally schedule someone for a round they've already completed.

Is this useful for HR consultants who don't do full-cycle recruiting?

Absolutely. The template is modular — HR consultants use the client tracker, meeting log, and project management sections while ignoring the full candidate pipeline. You use what fits your workflow and leave the rest.
