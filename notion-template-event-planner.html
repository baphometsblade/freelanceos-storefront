Frequently Asked Questions

Questions From Event Planners

Can I manage multiple events at the same time in Notion?

Yes. The event master tracker is a database of all your events — each with its own record, status, date, client, budget, and linked vendors. You can see all events in various stages simultaneously and drill into any one for the full detail view.

How does the run-of-show timeline builder work?

The run-of-show is a time-blocked Notion page template for each event — every slot has a time, activity description, responsible person, and status. You share it with your team and vendors so everyone is working from the same document on event day.

Can I track vendor contacts and contracts in the same workspace?

Yes. The vendor database holds every vendor's contact info, specialty, contract status, payment terms, and linked events. You'll always know who's booked, who's confirmed, and who still owes you a signed contract.

Does the template include budget tracking per event?

Yes. Each event has its own budget tracker with line items, actual vs. estimated spend, and remaining budget. You can see total event cost at a glance and know exactly where you stand against the client's budget before the final invoice.
