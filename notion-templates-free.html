Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about free Notion templates and how to get started today.

Can I use Notion templates for free? +
Yes — Notion itself has a free personal plan that supports unlimited pages and blocks. All FreelanceOS free templates work on the Notion free plan. You simply duplicate the template into your Notion workspace (one click) and start using it immediately. No Notion subscription required for the free templates. FreelanceOS Pro templates also work on the free Notion plan — the only Notion paid feature you might eventually want is sharing pages publicly or adding team members, but for solo freelancers the free plan is fully sufficient.
How do I duplicate a Notion template? +
Duplicating a Notion template takes three steps: (1) Click the template link to open it in your browser — it will open as a public Notion page. (2) Click the "Duplicate" button in the top-right corner of the page. (3) If you're logged into Notion, it will copy the template directly into your workspace. If you're not logged in, you'll be prompted to sign in or create a free account first. The entire process takes about 60 seconds. All FreelanceOS templates include setup notes to get you started in under 5 minutes.
Are these Notion templates compatible with the Notion free plan? +
Yes, completely. All FreelanceOS templates — free and Pro — are built to work with Notion's free plan. The free plan includes unlimited pages and blocks, which is all you need for client CRMs, project trackers, habit trackers, and financial dashboards. The only features that require a paid Notion plan are sharing pages with custom domains, adding multiple team members (more than 1 guest on the free plan), and some advanced permissions. For solo freelancers, the free plan covers everything you need.
What's the difference between free and Pro Notion templates? +
The free templates are standalone — each template works independently, and you set up the connections manually. FreelanceOS Pro is a connected system — all 50+ templates are linked in a single Notion workspace. When you add a new client to your CRM, it automatically appears in your project tracker, invoice database, and proposal pipeline. The Pro system also includes advanced dashboards (rolling revenue, client profitability, workload view), a 20+ SOP library with documented processes, geo-specific tax calendars (CRA for Canada, HMRC for UK, ATO for Australia), and all future template updates as FreelanceOS evolves.
Can I edit and customize these Notion templates? +
Yes — all Notion templates are fully editable once duplicated into your workspace. You can rename databases, add or remove properties, change colours, adjust views, and modify any content. The templates are starting points, not locked systems. Many freelancers duplicate the free starter kit, spend 30 minutes customizing it to their specific service offering, and use it for years. FreelanceOS Pro includes a setup guide and video walkthrough to help you configure the workspace to match your business in under an hour.
Do I need to know how to use Notion to benefit from these templates? +
No prior Notion experience required. Each template includes setup instructions within the page itself. Notion's interface is intuitive — it works like a mix of a document and a spreadsheet, and most freelancers are productive within 30 minutes of their first use. The FreelanceOS free templates are especially beginner-friendly: start with the Daily Planner and Client CRM first (the two highest-impact templates for new users), and add the others as you get comfortable. If you get stuck, FreelanceOS Pro includes a setup guide video.
Are the Notion templates a one-time purchase or subscription? +
One-time purchase — no subscription, ever. You pay once for FreelanceOS Pro and get lifetime access, including all future template updates and additions. The free templates are permanently free.
