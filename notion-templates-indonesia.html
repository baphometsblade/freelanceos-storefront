Freelancing in Indonesia: The Landscape in 2026

Indonesia has one of Southeast Asia's largest and fastest-growing freelance markets, with an estimated 65 million gig and independent workers. The combination of a young, digital-native population, high smartphone penetration, and a rapidly developing startup ecosystem has made Indonesia a major source of freelance talent for both domestic companies and international clients.

Key freelance hubs include Jakarta (the largest market, particularly strong in digital marketing, tech, and consulting), Bandung (growing rapidly as a UI/UX design and software development center — home to many startups and tech companies), Surabaya (second-largest city, growing digital services sector), and Bali (the creative hub for design, photography, video, and remote workers from both Indonesia and abroad who are drawn to the lifestyle).

For local work, Sribu.com is the dominant Indonesian freelance marketplace particularly for design, logo creation, and web development. Projects.co.id covers a broader range of project types. Fastwork, which is popular across Southeast Asia, has strong traction in Indonesia for SEO, writing, and design services. For international work, Fiverr and Upwork are widely used — many Indonesian freelancers earn in USD through these platforms, which offers significant income advantages given the IDR/USD exchange rate.

The gig economy is also expanding through e-commerce affiliate programs: Tokopedia Affiliate, Shopee Affiliate, and Lazada Affiliate have created new income opportunities for content creators and micro-influencers. Fastest-growing freelance sectors in Indonesia include UI/UX design, content writing in both Bahasa Indonesia and English, digital marketing and SEO, web and mobile development, and video production.

Indonesia's payment infrastructure is highly fragmented. Domestic B2B payments run primarily through bank transfer (BCA, BNI, BRI, and Mandiri are the dominant banks). Consumer-to-business payments increasingly use GoPay (from Gojek), Dana, and OVO. For receiving international payments, Wise (formerly TransferWise) has become popular for its low fees and multi-currency accounts, while PayPal and Stripe handle international card payments.

Tips untuk freelancer Indonesia: Untuk freelancer yang menerima pembayaran dari klien luar negeri dalam USD atau EUR, gunakan Wise atau PayPal untuk menerima pembayaran internasional, kemudian catat setara IDR di Notion menggunakan nilai tukar pada hari penerimaan (gunakan kurs Bank Indonesia). Ini memudahkan pelaporan PPh tahunan karena semua penghasilan harus dilaporkan dalam IDR dalam SPT tahunan kamu.

PPh Tax Considerations for Indonesian Freelancers

Indonesian freelancers are subject to Pajak Penghasilan (income tax). For self-employed individuals, the relevant tax is PPh Pasal 25/29, which is based on annual net income after deductible business expenses. If you provide services to Indonesian companies, they may withhold PPh Pasal 23 (2% of the gross service fee) directly from your payment — you'll receive a certificate (Bukti Potong) that you can offset against your annual tax liability. Maintaining your NPWP (Nomor Pokok Wajib Pajak) is required for tax reporting and increasingly required by clients before paying invoices.

FreelanceOS Pro helps you maintain the records needed for clean tax preparation — income by source, business expenses, withholding tax certificates received — but is not a substitute for a qualified konsultan pajak or akuntan for official filings.

How do I track USD income in IDR for Indonesian tax purposes?
In your Notion income database, add a 'Currency' property (USD / IDR / EUR) and an 'IDR Equivalent' number property. When you receive a USD payment, log the USD amount and the IDR equivalent using the exchange rate on the date of receipt (Bank Indonesia official rate is commonly used for PPh reporting). This makes your annual SPT preparation much easier, as you'll have a clean record of all foreign income already converted to IDR. Always consult a pajak consultant for your specific situation.
Can I manage international and local clients together in Notion?
Yes — this is one of Notion's core strengths for Indonesian freelancers who work across both markets. Your Client database can include a 'Client Type' property (Local / International), a 'Payment Method' property (Bank Transfer BCA/BNI/Mandiri / GoPay / Dana / OVO / PayPal / Wise / Stripe), and a 'Currency' property. Filtered views then let you see your local client pipeline and your international client pipeline separately, or together for a complete business overview.
Does FreelanceOS help with PPh freelance tax in Indonesia?
FreelanceOS Pro's income and expense tracking system helps you organize the records you need for PPh (Pajak Penghasilan) reporting. Log all income (IDR and foreign currency converted to IDR), track business expenses, and maintain records of your NPWP number and client NPWP for withholding purposes. Notion is an organizational tool, not tax software — always use a qualified konsultan pajak for official filings.
Can I track multiple payment platforms like GoPay, PayPal, and bank transfer in Notion?
