Can I track my product inventory and COGS with this template?
Yes. The Product Inventory & Supplies Tracker covers product name, brand, category, quantity on hand, cost per unit, reorder threshold, and supplier. The Business Dashboard shows product cost as a percentage of revenue so you can track COGS against the industry benchmark of 10-20% and make purchasing decisions accordingly.
Can I use this to plan my social media content?
