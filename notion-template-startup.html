Frequently Asked Questions

Questions From Founders

Can I use this Notion startup template with my co-founder?

Yes. FreelanceOS Pro is designed for collaborative use. You can share the Notion workspace with your entire founding team, set page permissions per section, and everyone works from the same source of truth. The team wiki and meeting notes database are especially useful for distributed teams.

Does the template include an OKR tracker?

Yes. The OKR Tracker lets you define company-level, team-level, and individual objectives with linked key results. Progress bars update as you check off milestones, so you always know how close you are to hitting quarterly goals.

How does the investor update template work?

The Investor Update template is a structured page format covering MRR, burn rate, key wins, key challenges, and asks. You fill in the data each month and it generates a clean update you can share directly with investors via a Notion link — no formatting time required.

Is there a burn rate calculator included?

Yes. The Burn Rate Calculator lets you log monthly expenses by category, set your cash runway target, and calculate how many months of runway you have remaining. It updates automatically as you add expenses.

Can I track hiring and recruitment inside Notion?

Yes. The Hiring Pipeline database tracks every open role from job description through offer stage. Each candidate is a linked record with interview notes, scorecards, and decision logs so your whole team stays aligned during the hiring process.

Does the product roadmap support different views?

Yes. The product roadmap database includes board, timeline, and table views. Filter by quarter, by team, or by feature status. Link roadmap items to OKRs so every feature has a clear strategic reason behind it.

Do I need any Notion experience to use this template?

No. The template comes with a setup guide that walks you through customizing each database for your startup in under two hours. If you know how to use a spreadsheet, you can use FreelanceOS Pro.
