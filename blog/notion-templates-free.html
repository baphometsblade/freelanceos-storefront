How to Duplicate Any Notion Template (3-Step Process)

Before diving into the list, here is exactly how to use any Notion template you find online:

  1. Open the template link. Every Notion template has a shareable public URL. Click it and the template opens in your browser, even if you are not logged in to Notion.
  2. Click "Duplicate" in the top-right corner. When you are viewing a Notion page, the top-right corner shows a "Duplicate" button (you may need to click the three-dot menu on mobile). Click it.
  3. Choose your workspace. If you are logged in, Notion will ask which workspace to copy the template into. Select your workspace, click confirm, and the template appears in your sidebar immediately.

Once duplicated, the template is entirely yours. Edit, delete, rename, and rearrange anything. Your changes will not affect the original. If you are not logged in, Notion will prompt you to sign in or create a free account before duplicating.

Pro tip: Notion's free plan supports unlimited blocks and lets you duplicate any template. You do not need a paid plan to use any of the templates in this list.
