With this many options, the choice paralysis is real. Here is a simple filter to find your starting point:

The single most important thing when choosing a free Notion template: pick one and use it for at least two weeks before evaluating whether to switch. The most common Notion mistake is template hopping — downloading a new template every week because the current one feels imperfect. Imperfect systems that you use beat perfect systems that you're still setting up.
