What Makes a Great Freelance Notion Template

Before we dive in, here's the criteria we used to evaluate each template:

  • Does it solve a real problem? Templates that look impressive but don't change how you work on a Tuesday afternoon get cut.
  • Is it ready to use immediately? You should be able to duplicate a template and start using it within an hour — not spend a week setting it up.
  • Does it use Notion features properly? Linked databases, filtered views, and formulas should be doing actual work — not just decoration.
  • Can it grow with your business? The best templates scale from "just starting out" to "juggling 8 clients" without needing a complete rebuild.
