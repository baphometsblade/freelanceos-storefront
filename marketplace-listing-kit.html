Part 1: Payhip Account Setup

One-time setup — takes about 5 minutes

Why Payhip? Payhip has a built-in marketplace with organic search traffic. Customers actively search for "notion template freelance", "productivity system notion", and similar queries. A free account lets you list unlimited products with a 5% transaction fee. Upgrade to Plus ($29/mo) or Pro ($99/mo) to eliminate the fee — worth it once you hit $600+/month in sales.
1

Create your account

Go to payhip.com and click "Sign Up Free". Use your business email (markmma1985@gmail.com or a dedicated business address). Choose a store username — something like baphometsblade or freelanceos to match your brand.

2

Complete your store profile

Go to Account → Store Settings. Fill in:

  • Store name: Baphometsblade / NotionOS Templates
  • Store description: Professional Notion template systems for freelancers, content creators, and agencies. One-time purchase, lifetime access.
  • Store logo: Upload your existing storefront logo
  • Social links: Add your storefront URL: https://baphometsblade.github.io/freelanceos-storefront/
3

Connect payment method

Go to Account → Payment Settings. Connect either PayPal or Stripe to receive payouts. Payhip supports both. If you already use Stripe for your other products, connect the same Stripe account for consistent reporting.

4

Set your affiliate program (optional but recommended)

Go to Marketing → Affiliate Program. Enable it and set commission to 30%. This gives Payhip affiliate marketers an incentive to promote your products organically. It's free traffic.

5

Prepare your product files

For each product on Payhip, you'll deliver a PDF or ZIP containing the Notion template link. Create a simple PDF (1-2 pages) for each product that includes:

  • The Notion template duplicate link
  • A quick-start guide (3-5 bullet points)
  • Support contact: markmma1985@gmail.com
  • Promo code LAUNCH20 for 20% off other products
6

Add your first product

From the dashboard, click "Add Product" → "Digital Download". You'll fill in: title, description, price, tags, cover image, and upload your product file. Use the exact copy below for each product.

Important: When uploading to Payhip, set the "Product Type" to Digital Download. Under the file upload section, you can also add the Notion template link as a redirect URL — this lets buyers access instantly after purchase without downloading. Use both the PDF file AND the redirect URL for best customer experience.
