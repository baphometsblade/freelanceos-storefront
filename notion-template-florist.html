Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use this for both wedding floristry and everyday flower shop orders?
Yes. The Event & Wedding Tracker handles large events, while the Client CRM and Quote Log work equally well for everyday orders, sympathy arrangements, and corporate accounts. You use the sections that fit your business model — the template is fully customizable.
How does the arrangement recipe log work?
Each recipe entry stores the arrangement name, flower list, stem counts, proportion notes, vase or mechanic details, and cost of goods. Over time your recipe book becomes a searchable library of your signature designs — with cost data that makes quoting faster and margins more consistent.
Can I track multiple weddings at different stages?
Yes. The Event & Wedding Tracker is a database where each row is a wedding or event. You can filter by status — consultation, confirmed, production, delivered — and see your entire upcoming pipeline at a glance, sorted by event date.
Does the supplier directory help me compare pricing across vendors?
The Flower Inventory Reference and Supplier Directory store pricing notes per vendor, minimum order requirements, lead times, and specialties. It's a reference system — not an automated price comparison tool — but having all the details in one place speeds up ordering significantly and reduces the back-and-forth when you're placing a rush order.
Can I share consultation prep notes with brides before their meeting?
Notion pages can be shared publicly with a link, so you can share a consultation questionnaire or mood board page with clients. Your main workspace stays private while you share specific pages selectively — a great way to look professional without building a separate client portal.
