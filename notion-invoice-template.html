How to Build This in Notion

You can have this invoice tracker running in under 10 minutes. Here is exactly how to set it up from scratch:

1

Create a new database

In Notion, type /database and choose “Table — Full Page.” Name it Invoice Tracker. This is your central invoice hub.

2

Add your properties

Add: Client (Text), Project (Relation), Rate (Number — dollar), Hours (Number), Status (Select: Unpaid / Paid / Overdue), Due Date (Date).

3

Add the Total formula

Create a Formula property called Total. Enter: prop("Rate") * prop("Hours") — Notion auto-calculates every time you fill in Rate and Hours.

4

Create filtered views

Add an “Unpaid” view (Status = Unpaid) and a “This Month” date filter to track monthly revenue. Your invoicing system is now live.
