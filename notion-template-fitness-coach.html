Can I manage multiple clients and their individual programs in one Notion workspace?

Yes. FreelanceOS Pro includes a client database where each client has their own record — current program, progress notes, measurements, goals, and session history. You can build individual programs, track progress per client, and see your full roster at a glance.

How does the progress tracker work for online coaching clients?

The progress tracker logs measurements, check-in notes, and goal status per client over time. Each entry is dated so you can see trends, compare to their starting point, and share updates during check-in calls. It works perfectly for remote and online coaching clients.

Can I track packages and invoices for my coaching business?

Yes. The invoice and package system lets you log each client's package type, payment status, package start and end dates, and remaining sessions. You'll always know who's paid, who's due, and whose package is about to expire.

Does FreelanceOS Pro work for in-person personal trainers or just online coaches?

It works for both. Whether you're training clients in person at a gym, coaching online, or doing a hybrid model — the template adapts. In-person trainers use the session scheduler and session notes; online coaches lean more on the check-in form database and progress tracking.
