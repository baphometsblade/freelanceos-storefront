Frequently Asked Questions

Are these Notion templates really free?
Yes. FreelanceOS Lite and CreatorHQ Lite are completely free — not trials, not demos. You get a fully functional Notion template you can duplicate to your workspace immediately. No signup, no credit card, no time limits.
What's the difference between Lite (free) and Pro ($29)?
Lite includes the core databases and views you need to get started. Pro adds advanced modules like a full CRM, revenue dashboard, templates library, and quarterly review framework. Most people start with Lite and upgrade when they want more depth.
Do I need a paid Notion plan?
No. Both templates work perfectly on Notion's free plan. You only need a paid Notion plan for features like unlimited file uploads or team permissions.
How do I get the template into my Notion workspace?
Click the "Get" button, then click "Duplicate" on the Gumroad page. The template will be copied directly into your Notion workspace. The whole process takes about 2 minutes.
