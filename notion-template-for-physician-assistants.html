Rotation Log & Clinical Hours Tracker

Document every clinical rotation with site name, specialty, preceptor, start and end dates, total hours logged, and rotation evaluation scores. PANRE & CME Credit Planner

Track every continuing medical education activity with category (Category 1 or 2), hours earned, provider, topic, completion date, and whether it satisfies PANRE-LA (Longitudinal Assessment) requirements. Job Search & Interview Tracker

Manage your entire PA job search from one organized pipeline. Track each position by practice name, specialty, location, application date, stage (applied, phone screen, interview, offer, declined), contact person, compensation range, and your personal notes on fit. Never lose track of an application or forget to follow up after an interview.

Certification & Licensing Renewal Calendar

Consolidate every renewal date that matters to your PA license — NCCPA PA-C certification (six-year cycle), DEA registration (three-year renewal), state PA license (varies by state), controlled substance license, and DEA state registration. See all renewal dates in one calendar view with 90-day, 60-day, and 30-day alerts so you never let a credential lapse.
