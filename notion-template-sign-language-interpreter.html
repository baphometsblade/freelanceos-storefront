What's Inside

Six Modules Built for Freelance Sign Language Interpreters

🗣

Assignment Tracker & Log

Per assignment: date, duration, setting/domain (legal/medical/educational/conference/performing arts/community/VRS/VRI/mental health), assignment type (on-site/remote/VRS/VRI), agency or direct client, rate (hourly or flat), travel pay or mileage, team interpreting (y/n), co-interpreter name, invoice number, invoice sent, payment received (y/n), days to payment, performance notes. Your complete assignment history and billing record by year.

📝

Assignment Preparation Log

Per assignment prep: assignment ID, domain and setting, materials received (briefs/slides/documents/prior transcripts), terminology research completed (subject-specific vocabulary lists, speaker's background, industry jargon), co-interpreter contact and team rotation plan, consumer preferences received, site logistics (parking/access/check-in procedure). Post-assignment debrief: new vocabulary added to glossary, what to research more deeply, feedback from co-interpreter.

🌟

Specialty Domain Tracker

Each specialty domain you're developing or active in: legal, medical, mental health, performing arts/theater, conference/keynote, educational K-12, higher education, DeafBlind, CDI collaboration, technical/STEM, government, religious. Per domain: assignment count, years of experience, related training completed, terminology resources built, RID SC:L credential if applicable, current rate for that specialty, whether you actively market it.

🏅

RID Certification & CEU Log

Credentials held (NIC, CI, CT, NIC Advanced, NIC Master, CDI, SC:L, OTC — and year earned), current 4-year maintenance cycle dates, CEUs required (8.0 per cycle), CEUs completed by category (professional studies/general studies per RID requirements), upcoming workshops and training planned, RID membership status and renewal date, state-level licensure requirements (many states have additional CE requirements beyond RID). Your complete credential record.

🏠

Agency & Direct Client CRM

Agency name, coordinator contact, rate on file, assignment types accepted, payment terms (net-30/net-60/on receipt), average days to payment, preferred communication method, year-to-date revenue, notes on working relationship. Direct client organizations: organization name, contact, role, rate negotiated, assignment volume, contract if any, renewal date. Preferred co-interpreter contact library: interpreter name, specialties, contact info, work history together.

💼

Interpreter Business Dashboard

Assignments this month (total and by domain), billable hours, monthly revenue, revenue by agency vs. direct client, average hourly rate (all assignments), average rate by domain (specialty premium visible), outstanding invoices, revenue vs. monthly income goal, CEU cycle progress, RID membership status, specialty domains active, annual revenue goal and pace. Your practice at a glance.
