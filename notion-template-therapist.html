Questions From Therapists

Is Notion HIPAA compliant for storing client health information?

Notion is not a HIPAA-compliant platform and should not be used to store protected health information (PHI). FreelanceOS Pro is designed for the business side of your practice — scheduling, billing, referral tracking, and business organization. Use a HIPAA-compliant EHR system for clinical notes and client health records.

What can a therapist safely use Notion for in their practice?

Therapists can safely use Notion for non-clinical business operations — appointment scheduling, billing and invoice tracking, referral management, intake coordination workflows, and practice administration. Keep all protected health information in your HIPAA-compliant clinical system.

How does the appointment scheduler work in Notion?

The appointment scheduler is a calendar database of upcoming sessions — client name, date, time, session type, and status. It gives you a clean overview of your week and helps you spot gaps, double-bookings, and upcoming renewals — without storing clinical content.

Does FreelanceOS Pro help with billing and insurance tracking?

Yes. The billing log tracks session fees, payments received, outstanding balances, and payment method per client. For insurance-based practices, you can log claim submission dates and reimbursement status to stay on top of outstanding insurance payments.
