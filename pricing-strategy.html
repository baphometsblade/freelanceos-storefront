Hourly Rate
Simple, transparent pricing. Bill for every hour you work.
Best for: ongoing work, consulting, uncertain scope
+ Easy to calculate, clients understand it
- Income capped by hours, penalizes efficiency
Project-Based
Fixed price per deliverable. Know your revenue before you start.
Best for: defined scope, repeatable work
+ Reward efficiency, predictable for clients
- Scope creep risk, hard to estimate initially
Retainer
Monthly fee for ongoing access and work. Predictable income, deeper relationships.
Best for: long-term relationships, stable income
+ Predictable revenue, deeper relationships
- Can feel like employment, scope boundaries crucial
Value-Based
Priced on client outcomes, not your time. Capture a share of the value you create.
Best for: measurable ROI work
+ Highest earning potential, aligned incentives
- Requires confidence, harder to sell initially
Productized Service
Standardized packages at fixed prices. Sell a solution, not your time.
Best for: scalability
+ Scalable, easy to sell, reduces scope discussions
- Less flexibility, may not fit all clients
