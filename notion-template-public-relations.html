Frequently Asked Questions

Is this Notion template built specifically for PR and communications professionals?
Yes — every database is designed around the PR professional's workflow: managing client retainers, building and maintaining media contacts, sending pitches, tracking coverage placements, and reporting results. It works for media relations, product launch PR, executive visibility programs, crisis communications, and thought leadership campaigns for freelance PR consultants and in-house communications teams alike.
Can I manage multiple PR clients with different campaigns at once?
Yes. The Client Campaign Tracker lets you manage multiple retainer clients simultaneously, each with their own campaign type, coverage targets, outlet tiers, and monthly status. You can filter the pitch pipeline and coverage log by client to see exactly where you stand for each account without losing context across campaigns.
How does the media contact database work?
The Media Contact Database holds one entry per journalist or editor with their beat, publication tier, contact email, relationship strength (cold through champion), recent coverage topics, and pitch success rate. You filter by beat, tier, or relationship strength to build targeted media lists for new pitches. Your contact database becomes more valuable with every campaign as relationship notes and pitch history accumulate.
Can I build monthly coverage reports for clients using this template?
Yes. The Coverage Report Builder provides a per-client, per-month template that captures placements by tier, total impressions, key wins, a coverage gallery with links, month-over-month trend, and a narrative summary of media achievements. It is designed to be client-report-ready with minimal additional formatting — export to a doc or share the Notion page view directly.
