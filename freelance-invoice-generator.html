3 Tips for Getting Paid Faster

1
Require 50% upfront. For any project over $500, ask for half before you start. This filters out time-wasters and protects you if the client disappears mid-project. Most serious clients expect it.
2
Use a specific date, not "Net 30". "Due by June 15" creates urgency. "Net 30" gets ignored. Write the actual date on the invoice. Clients pay specific dates; they forget vague terms.
3
Follow up on day 1 overdue — not day 7. A same-day follow-up ("Just checking the invoice arrived safely") signals that you track payments. Clients who know you're watching pay faster than those who think you won't notice.
